## The Great Calming and Contemplation Scroll 9 摩訶止觀卷第九

Description: Scroll 9 of the Chinese text of The Great Calming and Contemplation 摩訶止觀 from the Taishō Tripiṭaka. Spoken by Zhi Yi 智顗 (538—597) and written in Chinese by Guan Ding 灌頂 (561—632). This scroll includes a continuation of Section 6 Clarifcation of Means
