<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 894a

蘇悉地羯羅供養法

唐 善無畏譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 894A,

Susiddhikarapūjāvidhi (Su Xi Di Jie Luo Gongyang Fa),

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
More details on some of the rituals covered only briefly in T 893; contained within the group of 蘇悉地經類 Susuddhikara sūtra and related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 717-735 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 431')

None
