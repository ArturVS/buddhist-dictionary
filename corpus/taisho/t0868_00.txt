<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 868

諸佛境界攝真實經

唐 般若譯

共 3 卷

Volume 18, No. 868

Zhu Fo Jingjie She Zhenshi Jing

The Buddha Realms Emcompass True Reality Sutra

Translated by Prajñā in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Related to part 1 of the Sarvatathāgatatattvasaṃgraha (T 865). A text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taisho footnote for this sutra reads:

＜原＞縮刷大藏經, ＜甲＞嘉承二年寫高山寺藏本, ＜乙＞黄檗版淨嚴等校訂加筆靈雲校本塚本賢曉氏藏本, 黄檗版本以朱或墨校讐註記

Source: Shukusatsu daizōkyō [Reduced Print Canon]; 1. transcribed into the Takayama Temple edition in the second year of the Kajō era [1106-1108], 2. Revised and text added by Jōgon and et. al. to the Reiun collated version, Tsukamoto Zenryū family edition, and Ōbaku Canon use red or black ink for collation notes.
