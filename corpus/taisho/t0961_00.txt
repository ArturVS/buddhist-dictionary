<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 961

如意寶珠轉輪祕密現身成佛金輪呪王經

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 961;

Ruyi Bao Zhu Zhuanlun Mimi Xianshen Cheng Fo Jin Lun Zhou Wang Jing;

Mani Jewel Wheel Secret Body to Attain Enlightenment Gold Wheel Mantra Sutra;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text describes the story of the Mani Jewel Wheel, a picture of a mandala, mantras, and a homa (fire) ritual.

<h4>Notes</h4>
The source text has a picture of a mandala that is may not be shown in the NTI Reader correctly. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736].
