<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 939

佛說大乘觀想曼拏羅淨諸惡趣經

宋 法賢譯

共 2 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 939;

Fo Shuo Dasheng Guan Xiang Man Na Luo Jing Zhu E Qu Jing;

Mahayana Visualized Mandala for Purification of Evil Destinies Sutra;

Translated by Faxian in the Song in 2 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛儀軌類 ritual texts for buddhas (918-943).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 989-999 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1221')

None
