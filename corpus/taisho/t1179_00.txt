Volume 20, No. 1179;

Wenshushili Liu Zi Zhou Gongneng Fa Jing;

Manjusri Bodhisattva's Six Syllable Samartha Ritual Sutra;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text in the 文殊儀軌類 Manjusri ritual manual group (1171-1198).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞永暦元年寫東寺三密藏本, ＜乙＞久安五年寫高山寺藏本 Source: Source: Buzan College Canon published in first year of the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon version, first yeear of the Eiryaku era [1160-1161]; 2. manuscript from the Takayama Temple collection, fifth year of the Kyūan era [1145-1151].
