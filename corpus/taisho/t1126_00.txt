Volume 20, No. 1126;

Fo Shuo Puxian Man Na Luo Jing;

Samantabhadra Mandala Sutra;

Translated by Dānapāla in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes preparation of the mandala, performance of the ritual, visualization, and dharanis.
