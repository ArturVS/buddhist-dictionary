<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 1021

華嚴經心陀羅尼
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 1021;

Huayan Jing Xin Tuoluoni;

Avatamsaka Sutra Heart Dharani;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text is contained in the Sanjuji sakushi, a National Treasure of Japan.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞三十帖策子第二十二帖, 此經麗宋元明四本倶欠

Source: Twentieth sheet of the Sanjuji sakushi; this text is not contained in the Korean, Song, or Ming canons.
