<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 951

一字佛頂輪王經
唐 菩提流志譯

共 5 卷

Volume 19, No. 951
Yizi Fo Ding Lunwang Jing
Translated by Bodhiruci in the Tang in 5 scrolls

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 709 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 425')

<h4>English translations</h4>
None

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text is one of a group that describes a 一字 'one word' mantra, which is a magic word.
