<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 951

一字佛頂輪王經
唐 菩提流志譯

共 5 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 951;

Yizi Fo Ding Lunwang Jing;

One Syllable Buddhosnisa Wheel Turning Ruler Sutra;

Translated by Bodhiruci in the Tang in 5 scrolls

<h4>Other names</h4>
五佛頂經 Five Buddha Crowns Sutra; Sanskrit: Ekākṣara-buddhoṣṇīṣa-cakra-rāja-sūtra

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text describes the Buddha's place of enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree and describes the 一字頂輪王咒 'One Syllable Pinancle Wheel Turning Ruler Mantra'. The text is one of a group that describes a 一字 'one word' mantra, which is a magic word.
