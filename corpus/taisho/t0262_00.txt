<h4>Colophon</h4>
妙法蓮華經
姚秦 鳩摩羅什譯

共 7 卷

The Lotus Sūtra or Saddharmapuṇḍarīkasūtra (Miaofa Lianhua Jing)
Translated by Kumārajīva in 7 scrolls in the Later Qin

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 406 and Sanskrit title from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 116')

This is the popular translation of the Lotus Sūtra.

English translations: Kern (1884) [from Sanskrit], Tsugunari Kubo &amp; Akira Yuyama (2007), Reeves (2008), Watson (1993)

<h4>Primary Source</h4>
Kumārajīva, 《妙法蓮華經》 'The Lotus Sutra or Saddharmapuṇḍarīkasūtra (Miaofa Lianhua Jing),' in <i>Taishō shinshū Daizōkyō</i> 《大正新脩大藏經》, in Takakusu Junjiro, ed., (Tokyo: Taishō Shinshū Daizōkyō Kankōkai, 1988), Vol. 9, No. 262, Accessed 2016-08-03, <a href="http://tripitaka.cbeta.org/T09n0262">http://tripitaka.cbeta.org/T09n0262</a>.
