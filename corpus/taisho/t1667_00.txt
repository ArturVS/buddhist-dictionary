第 32 冊　No. 1667

大乘起信論
馬鳴菩薩造 唐 實叉難陀譯

共 2 卷

Volume 32, No. 1667
Treatise on the Awakening of Faith in the Mahāyāna
Composed by Aśvaghoṣa, translated by Śiksānanda in the Tang in 2 scrolls

<h4>Notes</h4>

Sanskrit title and date 695-700 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 623')
Also see Buswell and Lopez (Buswell and Lopez 2014, s.v. 'Aśvaghoṣa')
English translations: Aśvaghoṣa and Daisetz Teitaro Suzuki 2003, <i>The awakening of faith: the classic exposition of Mahayana Buddhism</i>, Mineola, New York: Dover Publications.
