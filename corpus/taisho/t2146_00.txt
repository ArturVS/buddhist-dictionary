<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 55 冊　No. 2146

眾經目錄

隋 法經等撰

共 7 卷

Volume 55, No. 2146

Zhong Jing Mulu

Sui Catalog of Scriptures

Composed by Fa Jing and others in the Sui in 7 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
The first catalog of the Chinese Buddhist canon to be produced in the Sui dynasty, requested by Emperor Wen.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 594 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1054')
