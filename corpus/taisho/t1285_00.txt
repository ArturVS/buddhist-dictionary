Volume 21, No. 1285;

Maṇibhadradhāraṇīsūtra (Fo Shuo Bao Xian Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Faxian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The yaksa king Manibhadra teaches a ritual for obtaining wealth.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 995-997 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1238b')
