摩登伽經
吳 竺律炎支謙共譯

共 2 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1300;

Mātangīsūtra (Modengjia Jing);

Translated by Zhu Luyan and Zhi Qian in the Wu in 2 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Tells the story of Prakṛti, a woman of the outcaste caṇḍālas, past lives, and astrological divination.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 307-313 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 766')
