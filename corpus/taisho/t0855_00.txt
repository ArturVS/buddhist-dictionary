Volume 18, No. 855,

Qing Long Si Gui Ji,

Qing Long Temple Ritual Manual,

One scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
青龍寺儀軌 Qing Long Temple Ritual Manual

<h4>Summary</h4>
A continuation of the description of rituals from T 852; text included within the 大日經類 Vairocana Sutra group of sutras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for T 855 reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera [temple] edition, printed in the first year of the Kyōwa era (1801).
