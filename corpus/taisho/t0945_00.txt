<h4>Colophon</h4>

大佛頂如來密因修證了義諸菩薩萬行首楞嚴經

唐 般剌蜜帝譯

共 10 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 945;

Da Fo Ding Rulai Mi Yin Xiu Zheng Liaoyi Zhu Pusa Wan Xing Shou Leng Yan Jing;

Great Might Arrived Bodhisattva’s Thinking-of-Buddhas as the Perfect Passage;

Translated by Pāramiti in the Tang in 10 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
A part of the Śuraṅgama sūtra
