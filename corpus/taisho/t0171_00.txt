太子須大拏經
西秦 聖堅譯

共 1 卷

Prince Sudana Sutra
Translated by Shengjian in the Western Qin in 1 scroll

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 388-409 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 207'). English title from Hsing Yun and Miao Guang trans. 2016, p. 373. Related to the Vessantara Jātaka in the Pali tradition.

English translations: none
