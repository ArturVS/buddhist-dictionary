火𤙖供養儀軌

Volume 18, No. 913,

Huo Xin Gongyang Yi Gui,

Fire Hūṃ Ritual, one scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of texts (908-914) relating to homa (fire) rituals and related procedures.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞豊山版, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本, ＜乙＞應保二年寫東寺三密藏本, （金剛頂護摩瑜伽供養火天）イ＋火＜乙＞

Source: Buzan edition; 1. Takayama Temple repository ancient manuscript, 2. Tō-ji Sanitsu canon manuscript dated second year of the Ōhō era [1161-1163] (Vajra Pinnacle Homa Yoga Offering to Agni)
