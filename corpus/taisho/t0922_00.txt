<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 922

藥師琉璃光如來消災除難念誦儀軌

唐 一行撰

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 922,

Yaoshi Liuli Guang Rulai Xiao Zai Chu Nan Niansong Yi Gui,

Liturgy for the Medicine Buddha of Pure Crystal Radiance Avoiding Calamities and Difficulties,

Composed by Yi Xing in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛儀軌類 ritual texts for buddhas (918-943).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞享保十九年刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞永久四年寫高山寺藏本, ＜乙＞高山寺藏古寫本, ＜丙＞端拱二年寫仁和寺藏本

Source: Buzan University canon published 19th year of the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Takayama temple canon manuscript dated 4th year of the Eikyū era [1113-1118]; 2. Takayama temple canon old manuscript; 3. transcript from the Ninna-ji canon dated 2nd year of the Duangong [cant' find era]
