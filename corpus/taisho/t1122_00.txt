<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1122

金剛頂瑜伽他化自在天理趣會普賢修行念誦儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1122;

Jingang Ding Yujia Ta Hua Zizai Tian Li Qu Hui Puxian Xiuxing Niansong Yi Gui;

Vajra Pinnacle Yoga Paranirmitavasavartin Guiding Principle Assembly Samantabhadra Cultivation Liturgy;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Gives pentasyllabic and heptasyllabic Chinese verse and short dharanis for the ritual.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:  ＜原＞明本, ＜甲＞三十帖策子第十九 Source: Ming canon; 1. Sanjuji sakushi, sheet 19.
