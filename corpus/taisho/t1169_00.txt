<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1169

佛說持明藏瑜伽大教尊那菩薩大明成就儀軌經

宋 法賢譯

共 4 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1169;

Fo Shuo Chi Ming Cang Yujia Da Jiao Zun Na Pusa Da Ming Chengjiu Yi Gui Jing;

Buddha's Sutra on the Great Enlightenment Achievement of Zunna Bodhisattva;

Dharani Yoga for the Great Teacher Cunda Bodhisattva Attainment Ritual;

Translated by Faxian in the Song in 4 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸菩薩儀軌類 bodhisattva worship ritual manual group (1119–1170).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 989-999 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1213')
