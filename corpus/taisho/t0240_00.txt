<h4>Colophon</h4>
實相般若波羅蜜經
唐 菩提流志譯

共 1 卷

Adhyardhaśatikāprajñāpāramitāsūtra
Translated by Bodhiruci in the Tang dynasty in 1 scroll.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated by Bodhiruci in 693, T 240. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 18')

English translations: none
