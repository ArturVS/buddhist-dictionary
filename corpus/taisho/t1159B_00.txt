<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1159B

佛說地藏菩薩陀羅尼經
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1159B;

Fo Shuo Dizang Pusa Tuoluoni Jing

Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva Dharani Sutra;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the vow to saved beings in hells, merits, and dharani of Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞東寺三密藏古寫本 Source: Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon old manuscript.
