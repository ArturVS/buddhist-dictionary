千手千眼觀世音菩薩大悲心陀羅尼

唐 不空譯

<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1064;

Ian Shou Qian Yan Guanshiyin Pusa Dabei Xin Tuoluoni;

Thousand Hand Thousand Eye Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva Great Compassion Heart Dharani

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
千手經 Thousand Hand Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
An alternative translation and detailed explaination of the 42 mudras and objects held, including images.
