<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1204

聖無動尊一字出生八大童子祕要法品
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1204;

Sheng Wu Dong Zun Yizi Chusheng Ba Da Tongzi Mi Yao Fa Pin;

Acalanatha One Syllable Eight Great Ritual Chapter;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Siddham script is included for the mantras in this text, included within in the 諸儀軌類 wisdom king ritual manuals group (1199–1243).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保二十年刊豐山大學藏本, ＜甲＞平安時代寫仁和寺藏本, ＜乙＞東寺三密藏古寫本 Source: Buzan College Canon published in the second year of the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Ninna-ji collection version, Heian Period Notes: [794-1192]; 2. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon old manuscript.
