<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 907

佛頂尊勝心破地獄轉業障出三界祕密陀羅尼

唐 善無畏譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 907,

Fo Ding Zun Sheng Xin Po Diyu Zhuan Yezhang Chu San Jie Mimi Tuoluoni,

Buddha Crown Worthy Supreme Heart able to Liberate from Hell, Transform Karmic Obstacles, and Transcend the Three Realms Secret Dharani,

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Related to T 899.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本

Source: Hase-dera canon, published in the first year of the Kyōwa era [1801]
