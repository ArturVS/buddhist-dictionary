<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 921

阿閦如來念誦供養法

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 921,

Achu Rulai Niansong Gongyang Fa,

Aksobhya Buddha Chanting and Offering Ritual,

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
阿閦供養法 Aksobhya Offering Ritual, 阿閦如來念誦法 Aksobhya Tathagata Liturgy, 阿閦念誦儀軌  Aksobhya Liturgy、阿閦軌 Aksobhya Ritual

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text describes a ritual wiht offering to Aksobhya Buddha, repentance, praise, transfer merit, and vows.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 746-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1313').

The text is mentioned in Scroll 15 of the 貞元新定釋教目錄 Zhen Yuan Era Catalog of Newly Authorized Buddhist Teachings (2157) with the title 阿閦如來念誦法.
