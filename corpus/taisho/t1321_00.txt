佛說施餓鬼甘露味大陀羅尼經

唐 跋馱木阿譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1321;

Fo Shuo Shi E Gui Ganlu Wei Da Tuoluoni Jing;

Amrta Hungry Ghost Offering Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Baduomua in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of texts relating to feeding hungry ghosts (T 1313-1321).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞鎌倉時代成賢寫東寺三密藏藏本 Source: Manuscript by Joken, Tō-ji Sanmitsu canon Kamakura period [1185–1333].
