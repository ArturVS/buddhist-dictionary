<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 864A

大日如來劍印

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 864A,

Dari Rulai Jian Yin,

Vairocana Sword Mudra,

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes chanting of matras and use of mudras; a text included within the 大日經類 Vairocana Sutra group of sutras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text contains Siddham characters that are not displayed in the NTI Reader. Please see <a href="https://21dzk.l.u-tokyo.ac.jp/SAT/satdb2015.php?">SAT</a> to view them.
