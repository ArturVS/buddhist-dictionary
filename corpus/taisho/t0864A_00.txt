<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 864A

大日如來劍印

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 864A,

Dari Rulai Jian Yin,

Vairocana Sword Mudra,

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes chanting of matras and use of mudras; a text included within the 大日經類 Vairocana Sutra group of sutras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon.

<h4>English translation</h4>
None
