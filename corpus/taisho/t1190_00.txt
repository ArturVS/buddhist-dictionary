<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1190

聖妙吉祥真實名經

元 釋智譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1190;

Sheng Miao Jixiang Zhenshi Ming Jing;

Manjusri's True Names Sutra;

Translated by Shi Zhi in the Yuan in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A eulogy to Manjusri in heptasyllabic Chinese verse.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:  ＜原＞明本 Source: Ming Canon.
