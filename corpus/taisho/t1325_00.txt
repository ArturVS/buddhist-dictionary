Arśapraśamanasūtra (Fo Shuo Liao Zhi Bing Jing)
Translated by Yijing in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Sūtra on the Treatment of Sores

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of texts relating to healing sickness (T 1324-1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
