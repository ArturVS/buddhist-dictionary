<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1101

佛說大方廣曼殊室利經

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1101;

Fo Shuo Dafangguang Manshushili Jing;

Mahavaipulya Manjusri Sutra;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes Avalokiteśvara's manifestation of different physical forms,predictions, emanation of Tara, short dharanis, mandala, ritual, and description of merits of the practice.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 746-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1276')
