無畏三藏禪要

唐 善無畏說

<br/>

Volume 18, No. 917,

Wuwei San Zang Chan Yao,

Śubhakarasiṃha’s Essentials of Meditation,

Spoken by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes initiation with repentance, recitation of vows, chanting of dhāraṇī, and meditation for recieving bodhicitta vows.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞縮刷大藏經, ＜甲＞平安時代寫東寺三密藏本, ＜乙＞黄蘗版性寂等校訂加筆本塚本賢曉氏藏, ＜丙＞黄蘗版
