<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1129

佛說金剛手菩薩降伏一切部多大教王經

宋 法天譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1129;

Śrīsarvabhūtaḍāmaratantrasūtra (Fo Shuo Jingang Shou Pusa Xiangfu Yiqie Bu Duo Da Jiao Wang Jing);

Translated by Fa Tian in the Song in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains a discourse between the Buddha and Vajrapani Bodhisattva. The text for many dharanis is given. 

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 994 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1202')
