<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1232

播般曩結使波金剛念誦儀

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1232;

Bo Ban Nang Jieshi Bo Jingang Niansong Yi;

Padanaksipa Vajra Liturgy;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:＜原＞享保年間刊豐山大學藏本, ＜甲＞平安時代寫仁和寺藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon version, Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Ninna-ji collection manuscript, Heian Period [794-1192].
