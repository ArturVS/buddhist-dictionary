<h4>Summary</h4>
The Vairocanāsaṃbodhi Sūtra is one of the foundational texts of East Asian Esoteric Buddhism. The initial format is a dialog between Vajrapāṇi Bodhisattva and Vairocana. Later sections describe visualization through a maṇḍala and ritual mudras.
