<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1031

聖觀自在菩薩心真言瑜伽觀行儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1031;

Sheng Guanzizai Pusa Xin Zhenyan Yujia Guan Xing Yi Gui;

Sacred Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva Heart mantra Yoga Contemplation and Practice Ritual Manual;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text describes establishing a mandala, entering it, ritual prostrations, mental state, recitation of a mantra, and mudras. The text is included within the 諸觀音儀軌類 Avalokitesvara ritual manuals (1030–1118) group.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1327')
