金剛壽命陀羅尼經法

唐 不空譯

<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1134A;

Jingang Shouming Tuoluoni Jing Fa;

Vajra Longevity Dharani Sutra Ritual;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Vairocana Buddha explains the Longevity Ritual and recites the dharanis. Siddham script and Chinese transliteration for the dharanis are given. See also T 1135.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text contains Siddham script not displayed in the NTI Reader. See <a href='https://21dzk.l.u-tokyo.ac.jp/SAT/satdb2015.php'>SAT</a> to view them. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞天永元年寫高山寺藏本 Source: Buzan University Canon published first year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804]; 1. manuscript from the Takayama Temple collection, first year of the Ten'ei era [1110-1113].
