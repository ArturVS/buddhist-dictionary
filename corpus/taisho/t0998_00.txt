佛說迴向輪經

唐 尸羅達摩譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 998;

Pariṇāmacakrasūtra (Fo Shuo Huixiang Lun Jing);

Translated by Śīladharma in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric ritual for transfer merit, including bodhisattva precepts and repentance.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 790 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1387')
