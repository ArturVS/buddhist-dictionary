普遍光明清淨熾盛如意寶印心無能勝大明王大隨求陀羅尼經

唐 不空譯

Volume 20, No. 1153;

Mahāpratisarādhāraṇīsūtra (Pubian Guangming Qing Jing Chisheng Ruyi Bao Yin Xin Wu Neng Sheng Da Ming Wang Da Sui Qiu Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 2 scrolls

<h4>Other names</h4>
Dhāraṇī of the Great Protectress who is Universally Radiant, Pure, Incandescent, a Wish-Granting Gem, and the Sealed Essence of the Invincible King of Mantras

<h4>Summary</h4>
A dhāraṇī and ritual relating to the goddess Mahāpratisarā, a wish fulfilling deity with many arms.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 720-774 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1349')
