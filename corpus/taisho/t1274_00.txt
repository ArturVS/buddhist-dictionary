Volume 21, No. 1274;

Da Sheng Huanxi Shuang Shen Pinayejia Tian Xingxiang Pin Yi Gui;

Ritual of the Image of the Dual-Bodied Deity Vinayaka;

Compiled by Jing Se in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of texts on Gaṇeśa (T 1267 - 1275).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和二年刊長谷寺藏本, ＜甲＞足利時代寫仁和寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, published in the second year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804]; 1. Ninna-ji collection manuscript, Ashikaga period [1338-1573].
