Volume 21, No. 1323;

Sarvarogapraśamanidhāraṇī sūtra (Chu Yiqie Jibing Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Sūtra on the Dhāraṇī to Eliminate all Illnesses; 除病經 Sūtra to Eliminate Illnesses

<h4>Summary</h4>
A very short text in a group relating to healing sickness (T 1324-1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 720-774 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1300')

<h4>English translation</h4>
Salguero, C. Pierce 2017, “Healing Dhāraṇīs: A Collection of Medieval Spells from the Taishō Tripiṭaka,” In: P. Salguero (ed.), <i>Buddhism and Medicine - An Anthology of Premodern Sources</i>, New York: Columbia University Press, pp. 292-303.
