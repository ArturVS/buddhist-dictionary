<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 867

金剛峰樓閣一切瑜伽瑜祇經

唐 金剛智譯

共 2 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 867,

Jingang Feng Louge Yiqie Yujia Yu Zhi Jing,

Diamond Pinnacle Yoga Teachers Sutra,

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 2 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Abbreviation: 瑜祇經 Yuqi Jing; 瑜伽瑜祇經 Yujia Yuqi Jing

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text thought to be composed in China, included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this sutras reads: ＜原＞明本, ＜甲＞三十帖策子第五, ＜乙＞縮册大藏經 Source: Ming Canon; 1. Sanjuji sakushi, no. 5; 2. Shukusatsu daizōkyō [Reduced Print Canon].
