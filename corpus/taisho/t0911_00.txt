<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 911

建立曼荼羅及揀擇地法

唐 慧琳集

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 911,

Jianli Mantuluo Ji Jian Zhai Di Fa,

Creating a Mandala and Selecting a Site for a Ritual,

Compiled by Hui Lin in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A collection of instructions on ritual procedures.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞縮刷大藏經, ＜甲＞黄檗版淨嚴等校訂加筆本塚本賢曉氏藏

Source: Shukusatsu daizōkyō [Reduced Print Canon]; 1. Ōbaku version with collation notes by Jōgon and others from a stack of documents courtesy of the Akatsuki family repository
