Volume 20, No. 1174;

Pañcākṣaradhāraṇīkārikā (Wu Zi Tuoluoni Song);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A five syllable dharani in praise of Mañjuśrī and accompanying Chinese verse.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 746-774 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1345')
