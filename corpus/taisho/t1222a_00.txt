<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1222a

聖迦抳忿怒金剛童子菩薩成就儀軌經

唐 不空譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1222A;

Sheng Jia Ni Fennu Jingang Tongzi Pusa Chengjiu Yi Gui Jing;

Wrathful Vajrakumara bodhisattva Attainment Ritual;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Includes Siddham script and Chinese transliteration for mantras. A text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243).

<h4>Notes</h4>
A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞麗本, ＜甲＞享和二年刊長谷寺藏本, ＜乙＞三十帖策子第二十五, ＜丙＞文治五年寫高山寺藏本, ＜丁＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本 Source: Korean Canon; 1. Hase-dera Canon version, second year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804]; 2. Sanjuji sakushi, 25th sheet; 3. Ōbaku Canon with annotations by Jōgon.
