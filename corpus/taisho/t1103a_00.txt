<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1103a

觀自在菩薩隨心呪經

唐 智通譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1103A;

Heart Mantra of the Responsive Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva (Guanzizai Pusa Sui Xin Zhou Jing);

Translated by Zhi Tong in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Provides the text of a long mantra. A text within the 諸觀音儀軌類 Avalokitesvara ritual manuals group (1030–1118).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 653 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 317')
