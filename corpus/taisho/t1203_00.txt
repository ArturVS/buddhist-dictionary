<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1203

聖無動尊安鎮家國等法
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1203;

Sheng Wu Dong Zun An Zhen Jia Guo Deng Fa

Acalanatha Ritual for the Peace of the Home and State;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the 安鎮法 Anchinhō 'Ritual for the Peace of the Home,' one of the 四箇大法 four great rituals of the Taimitsu school. Siddham script is included for mantras, as well as Chinese transliteration.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本, 原本校異中略符（イ）者秀陽阿闍梨本, （い）者宣雄比丘本, ＜甲＞大治三年寫東寺三密藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, published in the first year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804], which omits the イ and い strokes for some characters; 1. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon manuscript, third year of the Daiji era [1126-1131].
