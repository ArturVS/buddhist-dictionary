Volume 21, No. 1360;

Saṇmukhīdhāraṇīsūtra (Liu Men Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Xuanzang in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Dhāraṇī Sutra of the Six Gates

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸陀羅尼經類 dharani sutras group (1331–1420).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 645 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 447')
