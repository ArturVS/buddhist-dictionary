穢跡金剛禁百變法經

Volume 21, No. 1229;

Hui Ji Jingang Jin Bai Bianfa Jing;

Sutra of Hundred Transformations Skills of Ucchuṣma;

Translated by Ajitasena in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains images of 護身符 protective talismans. An esoteric text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243).
