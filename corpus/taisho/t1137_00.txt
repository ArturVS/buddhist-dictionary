佛說善法方便陀羅尼經

 失譯

Volume 20, No. 1137;

Sumukhanāmadhāraṇīsūtra (Fo Shuo Shanfa Fangbian Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Unknown in 1 scroll

<h4>Alternative names</h4>
善法方便陀羅尼咒經; 善方便經

<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha teaches expedient means and longevity dharanis, which have many merits.
