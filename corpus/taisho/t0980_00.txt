<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 980

大勝金剛佛頂念誦儀軌

唐 金剛智譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 980;

Da Sheng Jingang Fo Ding Niansong Yi Gui;

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text is one of a group of 諸佛頂儀軌類 Buddha crown ritual manuals (944–981).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞康元二年寫東寺三密藏本

Source: Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon, Kōgen era [1256-1257]
