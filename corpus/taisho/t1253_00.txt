Śrīmahādevīvyākaraṇasūtra (Da Jixiang Tian Nu Shi Er Qi Yibai Ba Ming Wugou Dasheng Jing);

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manuals group (1244–1330).
