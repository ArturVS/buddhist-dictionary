<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1032

瑜伽蓮華部念誦法

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1032;

Yujia Lianhua Bu Niansong Fa;

Yoga Lotus Division Liturgy;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text describes recitation of a mantra and mudras. It is included within the 諸觀音儀軌類 Avalokitesvara ritual manuals (1030–1118) group.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 746-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1325')
