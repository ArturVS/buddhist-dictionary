<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1051

佛說一切佛攝相應大教王經聖觀自在菩薩念誦儀軌

宋 法賢譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1051;

Fo Shuo Yiqie Fo She Xiangying Da Jiao Wang Jing Sheng Guanzizai Pusa Niansong Yi Gui;

Ritual for All the Buddhas Embracement of the Sutra of the King of Great Teaching that the Sacred Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva Recites;

Translated by Faxian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text within the 諸觀音儀軌類 Avalokitesvara ritual manuals group (1030–1118).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 989-999 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1222')
