<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 853

大毘盧遮那成佛神變加持經蓮華胎藏菩提幢標幟普通真言藏廣大成就瑜伽

唐 法全集

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 853,

Da Piluzhena Cheng Fo Shenbian Jia Chi Jing Lianhua Tai Cang Puti Chuang Biao Zhi Putong Zhenyan Cang Guangda Chengjiu Yujia,

Vairocana Sutra Lotus Matrix, Bodhi Banner Mantra Collection and Attainment Yoga,

Compiled by Fa Quan in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
A ritual manual associated with the Vairocana Sutra (T 848). 

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for the text reads: ＜原＞正徳二年刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞平安時代寫高山寺藏本 Source: Buzan College edition published in the second year of the Shōtoku era [1711]. 1. Takayama Temple edition, transcribed in the Heian Period.
