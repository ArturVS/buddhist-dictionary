佛說大摩里支菩薩經

宋 天息災譯

共 7 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1257;

Mārīcīdhāraṇīsūtra (Fo Shuo Da Mo Li Zhi Pusa Jing);

Translated by Tian Xi Zai in the Song in 7 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
In contrast to T 1254, this version gives greater detail on the iconography of Mārīcī as well as homa and other ritual elements.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 986-987 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1156')
