<h4>Colophon</h4>
金剛般若波羅蜜經
元魏 菩提流支譯

共 1 卷

Diamond Sūtra
Translated by Bodhiruci in the Northern Wei dynasty in 1 scroll.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated by Bodhiruci in 509, T 236b. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 14')

English translations: none
