第 20 冊　No. 1058

千手千眼觀世音菩薩姥陀羅尼身經

唐 菩提流志譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1058;

Nīlakaṇṭhakasūtra (Ian Shou Qian Yan Guanshiyin Pusa Lao Tuoluoni Shen Jing);

Translated by Bodhiruci in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
千手觀音姥陀羅尼身經 Sutra of the Divine Dharani of the Thousand Arm Avalokitesvara; 千手經 Thousand Hand Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text explains the merits of the Dharani, mudras, other ritual procedures, and miracles performed through the use of the thousand hands and thousand eyes.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 709 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 293')
