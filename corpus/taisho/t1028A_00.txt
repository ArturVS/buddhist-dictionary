<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 1028A

佛說護諸童子陀羅尼經

元魏 菩提流支譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 1028A;

Fo Shuo Hu Zhu Tongzi Tuoluoni Jing;

Protection of Children Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Bodhiruci in the Northern Wei in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text gives a dharani and a story where fifteen rākṣasīs protect children.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞麗本, ＜甲＞天平時代寫越前西福寺藏本, ＜乙＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本

Source: Korean canon; 1. Echizen Saifukuji collection manuscript, Tempyō period [729–749]; 2. Ōbaku Canon with nots from Jōgon and others.
