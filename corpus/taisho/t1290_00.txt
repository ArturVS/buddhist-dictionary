Volume 21, No. 1290
Yan Luo Wang Gong Xing Fa Cidi
Composed by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Includes Siddham text for mantras and an image for a mandala.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: 

＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本
