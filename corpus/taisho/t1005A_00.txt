<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 1005A

大寶廣博樓閣善住祕密陀羅尼經

唐 不空譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 1005A;

Mahāmaṇivipulavimānaviśvasupratiṣṭhitaguhyaparamarahasyakalparājadhāraṇī (Dabao Guangbo Louge Shan Zhu Mimi Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Alternative names</h4>
Secret Dhāraṇī of the Good Lord of the Great, Precious Pagoda; 寶樓閣經 Jeweled Tower Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text praises the virtue of the Pagoda, describes a dharani, homa ritual, and explains the benefits of the ritual, which includes erasing karma from sins.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞麗本, ＜甲＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本, 此經題新加

Source: Korean Canon; 1. Ōbaku Canon with notes by Jōgon; this text is newly added.
