<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 923

藥師如來觀行儀軌法

唐 金剛智譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 923,

Yaoshi Rulai Guan Xing Yi Gui Fa,

Medicine Buddha Contemplation Ritual Manual,

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛儀軌類 ritual texts for buddhas (918-943).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞享和二年刊長谷寺藏本, ＜甲＞東寺三密藏古寫本

Source: Hase-dera canon published 2nd year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804]; 1. Tō-ji Sanmitsu canon old manuscript
