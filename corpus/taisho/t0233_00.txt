<h4>Colophon</h4>
文殊師利所說般若波羅蜜經
梁 僧伽婆羅譯

共 1 卷

Mañjuśrī speaks the Prajñāpāramitā Sūtra
Translated by Saṅghabhara in the Liang dynasty in 1 scroll.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated by Saṅghabhara 僧伽婆羅 506-520, T 233 (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 11').

English translations: none
