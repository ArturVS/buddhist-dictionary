<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1263

氷揭羅天童子經

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1263;

Bingjieluo Tian Tongzi Jing;

Pingala Sutra;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Tells of Piṅgala, a the son of Hariti. Contains Siddham for five mantras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞保延三年寫高山寺藏本, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本, ＜乙＞寶壽院藏古寫本, ＜丙＞建長八年寫東寺三密藏本, ＜丁＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本 Source: Takayama Temple collection edition, third year of the Hōen era [1135-1141]; 1. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript; 2. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon version, ninth year of the Kenchō era [1249-1256]; 3. Ōbaku Canon with annotations by Jōgon.
