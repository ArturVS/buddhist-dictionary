<h4>Colophon</h4>
佛說開覺自性般若波羅蜜多經
宋 惟淨等譯

共 4 卷

Foshuo Kai Jue Zixing Boreluomiduo Jing
Translated by Wei Jing and others in the Song in 4 scrolls.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Translated by Wei Jing 惟淨 was in 1032. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 1485'; T 260)
