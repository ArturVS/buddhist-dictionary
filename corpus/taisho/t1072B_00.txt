Ma Tou Guanyin Xin Tuoluoni;

Hayagriva Heart Dharani;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Siddham text for the dharani. See also T 1072A.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text contains Siddham characters that are not displayed in the NTI Reader. See <a href='https://21dzk.l.u-tokyo.ac.jp/SAT/satdb2015.php'>SAT</a> to view the Siddham text. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞東寺三密藏古寫本 Source: Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon old manuscript.
