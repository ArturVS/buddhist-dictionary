不空羂索神呪心經

Volume 20, No. 1094;

Amoghapāśahṛdayasūtra (Bu Kong Juan Suo Shen Zhou Xinjing);

Translated by Xuanzang in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains a version of the 母陀羅尼真言 Mother Dhāraṇī Mantra from T 1090.
