<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 948

金輪王佛頂要略念誦法

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 948;

Jin Lun Wang Fo Ding Yao Lue Niansong Fa;

Golden Wheel Great Usnisa Short Liturgy;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text is one of a group of 諸佛頂儀軌類 Buddha crown ritual manuals (944–981).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Korean parallel from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1372')
