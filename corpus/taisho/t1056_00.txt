<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1056

金剛頂瑜伽千手千眼觀自在菩薩修行儀軌經
唐 不空譯

共 2 卷

Volume 20, No. 1056
Diamond Pinnacle Yoga Sutra of the Ritual Procedures of Thousand-Hand, Thousand-Eye Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva
Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 2 scrolls

Date 746-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1311')
