<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 930

無量壽如來觀行供養儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 930;

Wuliang Shou Rulai Guan Xing Gongyang Yi Gui;

Rites for Contemplation of and Offerings to Amitāyus Tathāgata;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text instructs the practitioner to receive consecration from the teacher, repent transgressions, perform dedication of merit, visualize Amitāyus, and invite him to be present.
