<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1079

七俱胝獨部法

唐 善無畏譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1079;

Qi Juzhi Du Bu Fa;

Goddess of Seven Koti Ritual;

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An expanded description of the ritual for the Cundi Dharani. See also T 1077.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, published in the first year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804 ].
