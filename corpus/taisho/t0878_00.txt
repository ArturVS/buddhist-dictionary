<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 878

金剛頂經金剛界大道場毘盧遮那如來自受用身內證智眷屬法身異名佛最上乘祕密三摩地禮懺文

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 878

Jingang Ding Jing Jingang Jie Dadao Chang Piluzhena Rulai Zi Shouyong Shen Nei Zheng Zhi Juanshu Fashen Yiming Fo Zuishang Cheng Mimi Sanmodi Li Chan Wen

Vajra Crown Tantra Vajradhatu Bodhimanda Vairocana Tathagata Sambhogakaya Inner Realization Wisdom Dharma Body, also known as the Supreme Buddha Vehicle Esoteric Liturgy

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.
