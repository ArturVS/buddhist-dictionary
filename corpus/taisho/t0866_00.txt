Sarvatathāgatatattvasaṃgraha,

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 4 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Visualizing and Reciting Method of the Abbreviated Diamond Pinnacle Yoga; Japanese: Kongōchō yugachū rakushutsu nenjukyō

<h4>Summary</h4>
Another translation of Sarvatathāgatatattvasaṃgraha; see T 865.
