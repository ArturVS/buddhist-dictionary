<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 866

金剛頂瑜伽中略出念誦經

唐 金剛智譯

共 4 卷

Volume 18, No. 866

Jingang Ding Yujia Zhong Lue Chu Niansong Jing

Sarvatathāgatatattvasaṃgraha

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 4 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Visualizing and Reciting Method of the Abbreviated Diamond Pinnacle Yoga

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts

Sanskrit title and date 723 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 429')
