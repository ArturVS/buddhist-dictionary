第 18 冊　No. 852a

大毘盧遮那成佛神變加持經蓮華胎藏悲生曼荼羅廣大成就儀軌供養方便會

唐 法全撰

共 2 卷

Volume 18, No. 852a

Da Piluzhena Cheng Fo Shenbian Jia Chi Jing Lianhua Tai Cang Bei Sheng Mantuluo Guangda Chengjiu Yi Gui Gongyang Fangbian Hui

Vairocana Sutra Lotus Platform Matrix Assembly Compassion Mandala Attainment Liturgy

Composed by Fa Quan in the Tang in 2 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
玄法寺儀軌

<h4>Summary</h4>
A ritual manual associated with the Vairocana Sutra (T 848).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon.
