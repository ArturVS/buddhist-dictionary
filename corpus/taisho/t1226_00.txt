<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1226

烏芻澁明王儀軌梵字

唐 空海請來

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1226;

Wu Chu Se Ming Wang Yi Gui Fan Zi;

Wisdom King Ucchusma Sanskrit Liturgy;

Requested of Kūkai in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Siddham text for a ritual in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual group (1199–1243).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: 平安時代淳祐寫石山寺藏本 Ishiyama-dera collection, manuscript by Shunnyū, Heian Period [794-1192].
