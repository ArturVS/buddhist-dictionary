<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1189

佛說文殊菩薩最勝真實名義經

元 沙囉巴譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1189;

Fo Shuo Wenshu Pusa Zui Sheng Zhenshi Mingyi Jing;

Eulogy to Manjusri;

Translated by Shaluoba in the Yuan in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha speaks words of praise for Mañjuśrī to Vajrapāṇi. The text is given in pentasyllabic Chinese verses of praise followed by a description of merits in prose.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞元本 Source: Puning Canon.
