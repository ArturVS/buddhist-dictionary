<h4>Colophon</h4>

佛說消除一切災障寶髻陀羅尼經

宋 法賢譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1400;

Fo Shuo Xiaochu Yiqie Zai Zhang Bao Ji Tuoluoni Jing;

Translated by Faxian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Teaches a dharani that can subdue the anger of asuras, dispell hunger, and eliminate epidemics and other harmful suffering.

<h4>Notes</h4>
