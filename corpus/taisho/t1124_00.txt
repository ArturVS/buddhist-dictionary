Volume 20, No. 1124;

Puxian Jingang Saduo Lue Yujia Niansong Yi Gui;

Samantabhadra Vajrasattva Short Yoga Liturgy;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Gives text for dharanis and instructions for the ritual.
