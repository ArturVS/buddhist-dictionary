第 19 冊　No. 963

佛說熾盛光大威德消災吉祥陀羅尼經

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 963;

Fo Shuo Chisheng Guang Da Weide Xiao Zai Jixiang Tuoluoni Jing;

Tejaprabha Great Majestic and Virtuous Averting Calamities Auspicious Dharani Sūtra;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Alternative names</h4>
大威德消災吉祥陀羅尼經 Yamantaka Jvalosnisa Dharani Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the Blazing Light Dharani to avoid disasters due to inauspicious star alignments.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞明本, 此經麗宋元三本欠

Source: Ming canon, this sutra is not in the Korean-Song-Yuan canons.
