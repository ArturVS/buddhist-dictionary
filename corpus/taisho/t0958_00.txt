<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 958

金剛頂經一字頂輪王儀軌音義

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 958;

Jingang Ding Jing Yizi Ding Lunwang Yi Gui Yin Yi;

Vajra Crown Sutra One Word Wheel Crown Liturgy;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text is one of a group that describes a 一字 'one word' mantra, which is a magic word.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本

Source: Hase-dera canon published in first year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804]
