<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1192

妙吉祥平等祕密最上觀門大教王經

宋 慈賢譯

共 5 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1192;

Miao Jixiang Pingdeng Mimi Zuishang Guan Men Da Jiao Wang Jing;

Wonderful, Universal, Secret and Highest Method for Meditation, Great Teaching King of Sūtras;

Translated by Ci Xian in the Song in 5 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric interpretation of the three vehicles and path to enlightenment.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:  ＜原＞明本 Source: Ming Canon.
