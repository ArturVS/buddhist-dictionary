<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1277

速疾立驗魔醯首羅天說阿尾奢法

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1277;

Suji Liyan Moxishouluo Tian Shuo Aweishe Fa;

The Āveśa Rite Explained by the God Maheśvara Which Swiftly Establishes Its Efficacy;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other Names</h4>
Āveśa Rite

<h4>Summary</h4>
Tells of the use of spirit medium in ritual possession, or āveśa, and divination.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1361')
