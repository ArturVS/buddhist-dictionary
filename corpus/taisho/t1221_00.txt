<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1221

青色大金剛藥叉辟鬼魔法

唐 空蜝述

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1221;

Qingse Da Jingang Yaocha Pi Gui Mofa;

Ritual of the Great Blue Vajra Yaksa, who Expels Demons;

Narrated by Kong Qi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Includes images and Siddham script. A text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞東寺寶菩提院三密藏藏本 Source: Tō-ji Hōbodai-in Sanmitsu Canon version.
