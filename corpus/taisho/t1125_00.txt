金剛頂瑜伽金剛薩埵五祕密修行念誦儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1125;

Jingang Ding Yujia Jingang Saduo Wu Mimi Xiuxing Niansong Yi Gui;

Ritual Procedures for the Practices and Chants of the Five Esoterica of Vajrasattva of the Yoga of the Adamantine Pinnacle;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes a ritual on entering the Mandala of the Five Secret Ones and texts of dharanis.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 746-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1318')
