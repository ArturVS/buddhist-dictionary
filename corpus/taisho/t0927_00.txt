<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 927

藥師七佛供養儀軌如意王經

清 工布查布譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 927;

Yaoshi Qi Fo Gongyang Yi Gui Ruyi Wang Jing;

Medicine Buddha and Seven Buddhas Liturgy and Sutra;

Translated by Gongbuchabu in the Qing in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛儀軌類 ritual texts for buddhas (918-943).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞清道光四年刊 Source: Published in the 4th year of the Daoguang era [1821-1850] of the Qing dynasty.
