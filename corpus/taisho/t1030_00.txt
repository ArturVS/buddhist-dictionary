<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1030

觀自在大悲成就瑜伽蓮華部念誦法門

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1030;

Guanzizai Dabei Chengjiu Yujia Lianhua Bu Niansong Famen;

Avalokitesvara Great Compassion Attainment Yogo Lotus Division Liturgy

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text teaches a dharani invoking Avalokitesvara and method to recite it with mudras. The text is included within the 諸觀音儀軌類 Avalokitesvara ritual manuals (1030–1118) group.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1374')
