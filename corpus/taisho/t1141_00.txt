<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1141

慈氏菩薩略修瑜伽念誦法

唐 善無畏譯

共 2 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1141;

Cishi Pusa Lue Xiu Yujia Niansong Fa;

Maitreya Abbreviated Yoga Liturgy;

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 2 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the ritual including mantras, mandala, and other chanting. Mantras are given in Siddham script and Chinese transliteration.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text contains images and Siddham script. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞寛治六年寫東寺三密藏本, ＜乙＞高山寺藏古寫本, ＜丙＞貞應元年寫高山寺藏本, ＜丁＞平安時代寫仁和寺藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon, published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon manuscript, 6th year of the Kanji era [1087-1094]; 2. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript; 3. Takayama Temple collection manuscript, 1st year of the Jōō era [1222-1224]; 4. Ninna-ji collection version, Heian period [794－1192].
