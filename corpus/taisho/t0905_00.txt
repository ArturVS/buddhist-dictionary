Volume 18, No. 905,

San Zhong Xi Di Po Diyu Zhuan Yezhang Chu San Jie Mimi Tuoluoni Fa,

Secret Dharanis for Three Kinds of Siddhi able to Liberate from Hell, Transform Karmic Obstacles, and Transcend the Three Realms,

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Related to T 899.
