馬鳴菩薩大神力無比驗法念誦軌儀

唐 金剛智譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1166;

Ma Ming Pusa Da Shenli Wubi Yan Fa Niansong Gui Yi;

Ritual Instructions for the Recitation of the Incomparably Efficacious Method of the Divine Power of Aśvaghoṣa Bodhisattva;

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A short text of Chinese origin.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本 Source: Buzan University Canon published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736].
