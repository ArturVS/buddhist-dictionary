<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1183

一髻文殊師利童子陀羅尼念誦儀軌

唐 金剛福壽譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1183;

Yi Ji Wenshushili Tongzi Tuoluoni Niansong Yi Gui;

The Boy Manjusri's Hair Bun Dharani Liturgy;

Translated by Jingang Fushou in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Siddham text and Chinese transliteration for mantras as well as Chinese verse for the liturgy.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保十七年刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞永久三年寫東寺三密藏本, ＜乙＞永保三年寫東寺三密藏本, ＜丙＞平安時代寫仁和寺藏本 Source: Buzan University Canon published in seventh year of the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon manuscript, third year of the Eikyū [1113-1118]; 2. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon version, third year of the Eihō era [1081-1084]; 3. Ninna-ji collection manuscript, Heian period [794-1192].
