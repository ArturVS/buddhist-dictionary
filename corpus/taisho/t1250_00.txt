<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1250

北方毘沙門多聞寶藏天王神妙陀羅尼別行儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1250;

Bei Fang Pishamen Duo Wen Baozang Tianwang Shen Miao Tuoluoni Bie Xing Yi Gui;

Vaisravana of the North Wonderful Dharani and Other Cultivation Liturgy;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Siddham script as well as Chinese transliteration is given for the mantras in this esoteric text in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manuals group (1244–1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞大日本續藏經, ＜甲＞平安時代寫仁和寺藏本, ＜乙＞延久六年寫本 Source: Dai Nippon Zokuzōkyō [Supplementary Buddhist Canon]; 1. Heian Period [794－1192] manuscript from the Ninna-ji collection; 2. manuscript, 6th year of the Enkyū era [1069-1074].
