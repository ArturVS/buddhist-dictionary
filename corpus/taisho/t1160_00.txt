<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1160

日光菩薩月光菩薩陀羅尼
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1160;

Riguang Pusa Yueguang Pusa Tuoluoni;

Suryaprabha Bodhisattva and Candraprabha Bodhisattva Dharanis;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Two short dharanis.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:  ＜原＞黄檗版淨嚴等校訂加筆本 Source: Ōbaku Canon with annotations by Jōgon.
