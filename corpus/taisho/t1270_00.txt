<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1270

大聖歡喜雙身大自在天毘那夜迦王歸依念誦供養法

唐 善無畏譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1270;

Da Sheng Huanxi Shuang Shen Dazizaitian Pinayejia Wang Guiyi Niansong Gongyang Fa;

Liturgy, Refuge, and Offering Ritual of King Vinayaka, the Dual-Bodied Mahasvara;

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Japanese: Daishōten Kangi sōshin Daijizaiten binayaka-ō kie nenzu kuyō hō

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric ritual associated with the deity Gaṇeśa to avoid misfortune and gain wealth.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和二年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, published second year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].
