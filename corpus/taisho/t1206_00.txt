<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1206

佛說俱利伽羅大龍勝外道伏陀羅尼經
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1206;

Fo Shuo Julijialuo Da Long Sheng Wai Dao Fu Tuoluoni Jing;

Dragon King Kulika Overcomes Outsiders Dharani Sutra;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A short dharani of the Dragon King Kulika, a manifestation of esoteric deity Acalanātha-Vidyārāja.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon version published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736].
