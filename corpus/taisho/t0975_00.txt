<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 975

白傘蓋大佛頂王最勝無比大威德金剛無礙大道場陀羅尼念誦法要
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 975;

White Canopy Great Buddha Crown Supreme Attainment Vajra Unobstructed Bohdimandala Dharani Liturgy (Bai Sangai Da Fo Ding Wang Zui Sheng Wubi Da Wei De Jingang Wu'ai Dadao Chang Tuoluoni Niansong Fa Yao);

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of three texts about the 白傘 White Canopy, one of the five 佛頂 Buddha Crowns; see T 976.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞延享元年刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本

Source: Buzan University Canon published first year of the Enkyō era [1744-1748]; 1. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript
