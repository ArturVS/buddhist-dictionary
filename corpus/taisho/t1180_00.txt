Volume 20, No. 1180;

Saḍakṣaravidyāmantrasūtra (Liu Zi Shen Zhou Jing);

Translated by Bodhiruci in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The six syllable mantra. This text is in the 文殊儀軌類 Manjusri ritual manual group (1171-1198).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 693 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 316')
