摩醯首羅天法要
 

共 1 卷

Volume 21, No. 1279;

Moxishouluo Tian Fa Yao

Maheshvara Ritual Outline;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manual group. (1244–1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞石山寺藏古寫本 Source: Ishiyama-dera collection old manuscript.
