佛說金剛香菩薩大明成就儀軌經

宋 施護譯

Volume 20, No. 1170;

Rāṣṭrapālaparipṛcchāsūtra (Fo Shuo Jingang Xiang Pusa Da Ming Chengjiu Yi Gui Jing);

Translated by Dānapāla in the Song in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸菩薩儀軌類 bodhisattva worship ritual manual group (1119–1170).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 994 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1207')
