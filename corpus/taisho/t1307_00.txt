<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1307

佛說北斗七星延命經

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1307;

Fo Shuo Bei Dou Qi Xing Yan Ming Jing;

Scripture Expounded by the Buddha on Prolonging Life Through Worship of the Seven Stars of the Northern Dipper;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains images and talismans. Related to T 1305 and T 1306.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:  ＜原＞享和二年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon, published in the second year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].
