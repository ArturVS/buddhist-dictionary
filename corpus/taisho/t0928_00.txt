<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 928

修藥師儀軌布壇法

清 阿旺扎什補譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 928;

Xiu Yaoshi Yi Gui Bu Tan Fa;

Medicine Buddha Mandala Ritual;

Translated by Awangzhashenbu in the Qing in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Includes images and also Siddham, and Tibetan script relating to the ritual text; one of a group of 諸佛儀軌類 ritual texts for buddhas (918-943).

<h4>Notes</h4>
The SAT version of the text contains images that are not shown in the NTI Reader. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞清道光四年刊 Source: Published in the 4th year of the Daoguang era [1821-1850] of the Qing dynasty.
