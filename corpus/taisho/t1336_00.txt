Volume 21, No. 1336;

Miscellaneous Collection of Dhāraṇīs (Tuoluoni Za Ji);

Translated by Unknown in 10 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
A collection of dhāraṇīs, incorporating many from T 1332. The dhāraṇīs have properties, such as fulfilling wishes, purging sins, and curing illness.
