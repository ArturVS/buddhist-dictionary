<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1251

吽迦陀野儀軌

唐 金剛智譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1251;

Hong Jia Tuo Ye Yi Gui;

Hum Syllable Ritual;

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manuals group (1244–1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: 久安三年寫東寺寶菩提院　密藏藏本 Tō-ji Hōbodai-in Sanmitsu Canon manuscript, third year of the Kyūan era [1145-1151].
