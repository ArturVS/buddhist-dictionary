<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 953

一字奇特佛頂經

唐 不空譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 953;

Uṣṇīṣacakravartitantra (Yizi Qite Fo Ding Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text is one of a group that describes a 一字 'one word' mantra, which is a magic word.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 746-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1277')

<h4>English translations</h4>
None
