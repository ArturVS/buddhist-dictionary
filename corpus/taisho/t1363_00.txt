<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1363

勝幢臂印陀羅尼經

唐 玄奘譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1363;

Dhvajāgrakeyūrā (Sheng Chuang Bi Yin Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Xuanzang in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
勝幢臂印經; 勝幢印經

<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha teaches the dharani to avoid evil rebirths and suffering and for the attainment of blessings and  joy.
