<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1311

梵天火羅九曜

唐 一行述

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1311;

Fantian Huoluo Jiu Yao;

Indian Astrology of the Nine Luminous Bodies;

Narrated by Yi Xing in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains images of deities in relation to celestial bodies and star charts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和二年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, published in the second year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].
