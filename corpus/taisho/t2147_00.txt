<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 55 冊　No. 2147

眾經目錄
隋 彥琮撰

共 5 卷

Volume 55, No. 2147

Zhong Jing Mulu

Sui Dynasty Renshou Era Catalog of Scriptures

Composed by Yan Cong in the Sui in 5 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
A catalog of the Chinese Buddhist canon produced in the Sui dynasty as requested by Emperor Yang.
