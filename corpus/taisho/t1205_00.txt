<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1205

勝軍不動明王四十八使者祕密成就儀軌

唐 遍智集

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1205;

Sheng Jun Budong Ming Wang Si Shi Ba Shizhe Mimi Chengjiu Yi Gui;

Acalanātha Vidyārāja and Forty Eight Messengers Secret Victory and Attainment Ritual;

Compiled by Bian Zhi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes mantras and their merits, images and visualization and the forty eight messengers of Acalanātha, who are protecting deities. 

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon published in the first year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].
