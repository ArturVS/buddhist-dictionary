Da Piluzhena Lue Yao Su Ji Men Wu Zhi Niansong Fa

Mahavairocana abbreviated Five Branch Healing Liturgy

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text included within the 大日經類 Vairocana Sutra group of sutras.
