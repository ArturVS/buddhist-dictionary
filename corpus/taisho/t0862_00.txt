<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 862

阿闍梨大曼荼攞灌頂儀軌
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 862,

Acarya Mandala Consecration Ritual Manual,

Asheli Da Man Tu Luo Guan Ding Yi Gui,

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes abhiseka consecration; a text included within the 大日經類 Vairocana Sutra group of sutras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for the text reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本, ＜甲＞平安時代寫覺海本高山寺藏本, ＜乙＞平安時代寫高山寺藏本, ＜丙＞承元二年行辨傳受本東寺三蜜藏本 Source: Hase-dera canon published in the first year of the Kyōwa [1801-1804], 1. transcribed by Kakukai into the Takayama Temple canon in the Heian Period [794－1192]; 2. received into the Tō-ji sanmitsu canon in the second year of the Jōgen era [1207-1211].
