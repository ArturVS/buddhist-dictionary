<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1111

青頸觀自在菩薩心陀羅尼經

唐 不空注

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1111;

Qing Jing Guanzizai Pusa Xin Tuoluoni Jing;

Nilakantha Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva Heart Dharani Sutra;

Recorded by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll;

<h4>Summary</h4>
Gives the text of the dharani.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞承安三年寫東寺三密藏本 Source: Buzan University Canon, published Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. manuscript from the Tō-ji collection, 3rd year of the Jōan era [1171-1175].
