<h4>Colophon</h4>

能淨一切眼疾病陀羅尼經

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1324;

Cakṣurviśodhanavidyā (Neng Jing Yiqie Yan Jibing Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Sūtra on the Dhāraṇī that can clear up all Eye Ailments

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of texts relating to healing sickness (T 1324-1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 746-774 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1301')

<h4>English translation</h4>
