<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1088

如意輪菩薩觀門義注祕訣
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1088;

Cintāmaṇicakra Bodhisattva Teaching of the Secret Method;

Ruyi Lun Pusa Guan Men Yi Zhu Mi Jue;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Includes mantras and esoteric ritual instructions. Part of the Sanjuji sakushi by Kūkai.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text contains Siddham characters that are not displayed in the NTI Reader. See <a href='https://21dzk.l.u-tokyo.ac.jp/SAT/satdb2015.php'>SAT</a> to view these. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞黄檗版淨嚴等校訂加筆本塚本賢曉氏藏, ＜甲＞三十帖策子第二十七 Source: Ōbaku Canon version with revisions and notes by Jōgon, Tsukamoto family collection; 1. 27th sheet of the Sanjuji sakushi.
