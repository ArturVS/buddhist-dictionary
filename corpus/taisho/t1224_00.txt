<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1224

金剛童子持念經
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1224;

Jingang Tongzi Chi Nian Jing;

Vajrakumara Liturgy;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞歴應三年寫東寺寶菩提院三密藏藏本 Source: Tō-ji Hōbodai-in Sanmitsu Canon, third year of the Ryakuō era [1338-1342].
