<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 1000

成就妙法蓮華經王瑜伽觀智儀軌
唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 1000;

Chengjiu Miaofa Lianhua Jing Wang Yujia Guan Zhi Yi Gui;

Ritual for Attainment of the Wonderful Lotus Sutra Yoga Contemplation;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
法華經觀智儀軌 Lotus Sutra Contemplation Ritual; 成就法華儀軌 Lotus Sutra Attainment Ritual; 法華觀智軌 Lotus Sutra Contemplation Ritual; 法華儀軌 Lotus Sutra Ritual

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric visualization ritual based on the Lotus Sutra, including verses of praise, creating and entering the mandala, visualization, blessing, mantras, and ritual offerings.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1343')
