佛說毘沙門天王經

宋 法天譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1245;

Aṭānāṭīyamasūtra (Fo Shuo Pishamen Tian Wang Jing);

Translated by Fa Tian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Vaisravana Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
Vaisravana and innumerable yaksas worship the Buddha. Empowered by the Buddha, Vaisravana recites mantras.
