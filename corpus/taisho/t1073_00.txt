何耶揭唎婆像法

Volume 20, No. 1073;

Hayagriva Image Ritual (Heyejielipo Xiang Fa);

One scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
賀耶揭哩婆儀軌 Hayagriva Ritual

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric ritual relating to the deity Hayagriva, of of the eight great wisdom kings. The text describes the image of Hayagriva and mantras for curing illness, mudras, keeping away evil spirits, and protection of the body.
