<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1091

七星如意輪祕密要經

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1091;

Qi Xing Ruyi Lun Mimi Yao Jing;

Scripture on the Secret Essentials of Cintāmaṇicakra and the Great Dipper;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text describes worship of the Big Dipper and Heavenly deities in a mixture of prose and heptasyllabic Chinese verse.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, first year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].
