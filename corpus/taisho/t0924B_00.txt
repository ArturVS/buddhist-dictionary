Volume 19, No. 924B,

Medicine Buddha Liturgy,

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
See T 924A.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞享保年間刊豐山大學藏本, ＜甲＞保延三年寫高山寺藏本, ＜乙＞高山寺藏古寫本

Source: Toyoyama University canon published in the Kyōhō era; 1. Takayama temple repository manuscript dated third year of the Hōen era [1135-1141]; 2. Takayama temple repository old manuscript
