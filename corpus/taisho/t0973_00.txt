尊勝佛頂脩瑜伽法軌儀

唐 善無畏譯

共 2 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 973;

Zun Sheng Fo Ding Xiu Yujia Fa Gui Yi;

Supreme Buddha Crown Yoga Cultivation Ritual Manual

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 2 scrolls

<h4>Other names</h4>
Variant writing: 尊勝佛頂修瑜伽法軌儀

<h4>Summary</h4>
See T 972.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豐山大學藏本, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本, ＜乙＞天治元年寫東寺三密藏本, ＜丙＞寛治八年寫仁和寺藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript; 2. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon, first year of the Tenji era [1124-1126]; 3. manuscript from Ninna-ji collection, 8th year of the Kanji era [1087-1095].
