<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1057b

千眼千臂觀世音菩薩陀羅尼神呪經

唐 智通譯

共 2 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1057b;

Qian Yan Qian Bi Guanshiyin Pusa Tuoluoni Shen Zhou Jing;

Sutra of the Divine Dharani of the Thousand Eye, Thousand Arm Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva;

Translated by Zhi Tong in the Tang in 2 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
See T 1056.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞明本, 此經與麗本異同甚繁故別載 Source: Ming Canon; this sutra is different than the version of the Korean canon.
