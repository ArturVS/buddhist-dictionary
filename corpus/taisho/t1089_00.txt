<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1089

都表如意摩尼轉輪聖王次第念誦祕密最要略法

唐 解脫師子譯

共 1 卷

Volume 20, No. 1089;

Dou Biao Ruyi Moni Zhuanlun Sheng Wang Cidi Niansong Mimi Zui Yao Lue Fa;

An Outline of all the Elements of the Cintāmaṇi Chakravarti Raja Recitation and Rituals;

Translated by Jietuo Shizi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A short summary of a number of different rituals. The text is divided into a number of parts: 1. 相貌 countenance, 2. 大心密言 Great Bodhi Secret Mantra, 3. 根本密言 Root Secret Mantra, 4. 小心密言 Lesser Minded Secret Mantra, 5. 成就世間出世間除災護摩念誦 Transcendental Attainment Homa Ritual for Avoiding Misfortune, 6. 增益福德 Increasing Blessing, 7. 隨心憶念 Wish Fulfilling Thoughts, 8. 阿毘遮噜迦 Abhicaraka (overcoming adversaries).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞平安時代寫東寺三密藏本

Source: Buzan University Canon version, published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. manuscript from Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon, Heian Period [794－1192]
