<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 925

藥師琉璃光王七佛本願功德經念誦儀軌

元 沙囉巴譯

共 2 卷

Volume 19, No. 925;

Yaoshi Liuli Guang Wang Qi Fo Benyuan Gongde Jing Niansong Yi Gui;

Medicine Buddha, King of Lapis Lazuli Light and Merits of the Seven Buddhas Sutra and Liturgy;

Translated by Shaluoba in the monetary unit in 2 scrolls

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞元本

Source: Puning Canon
