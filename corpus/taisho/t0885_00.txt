佛說一切如來金剛三業最上祕密大教王經

宋 施護譯

共 7 卷

Volume 18, No. 885

Fo Shuo Yiqie Rulai Jingang San Ye Zuishang Mimi Da Jiao Wang Jing

Guhyasamājatantra

Translated by Dānapāla in the Song in 7 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Literally meaning “Secret Assembly Tantra,” the Guhyasamājatantra is an important tantra in esoteric Buddhism, known as the “king of tantras” in Tibetan practice. It has an overt sexual nature.
