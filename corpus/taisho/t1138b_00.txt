<h4>Summary</h4>
See T 1137.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:  ＜原＞明本, 此經與麗本異同甚繁故別載 Source: Ming Canon; this version differs considerably from the Korean canon.
