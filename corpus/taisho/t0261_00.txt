<h4>Colophon</h4>
大乘理趣六波羅蜜多經
唐 般若譯

共 10 卷

The Sūtra on the Mahāyāna Practice of the Six Perfections
Translated by Prajña in the Tang in 10 scrolls.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Offered to the emperor in 788. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 1381'; T 261)
