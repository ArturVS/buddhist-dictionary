<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 965

大妙金剛大甘露軍拏利焰鬘熾盛佛頂經

唐 達磨栖那譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 965;

Da Miao Jingang Da Ganlu Jun Na Li Yan Man Chisheng Fo Ding Jing;

Wonderful Vajra Ambrosia Kundali, Yamantaka Blazing Buddha Crown Sutra

Translated by Damoqina in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text is one of a group of 諸佛頂儀軌類 Buddha crown ritual manuals (944–981).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豐山大學藏本 Source: Buzan University Canon published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736].
