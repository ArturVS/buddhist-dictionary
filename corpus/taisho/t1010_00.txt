<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 1010

佛說出生無邊門陀羅尼儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 1010;

Fo Shuo Chusheng Wubian Men Tuoluoni Yi Gui;

Scripture of the Sublime Grasp of the Immeasurable Portal;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Anantamukhasādhakadhāraṇī

<h4>Summary</h4>
See T 1009.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本, ＜甲＞三十帖策子, 此經麗宋元明四本倶欠 Source: Ōbaku Canon with notes by Jōgon; 1. Sanjuji sakushi. This text is not included in the Korean canon.
