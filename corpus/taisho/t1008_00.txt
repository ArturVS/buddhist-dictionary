<h4>Colophon</h4>

菩提場莊嚴陀羅尼經

唐 不空譯

Volume 19, No. 1008
Bodhimaṇḍalalakṣālaṁkāranāmadhāraṇī (Puti Chang Zhuangyan Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text in the group of 諸經儀軌類 sutra ritual manuals (982–1029).

Sanskrit title and date 746-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1299')
