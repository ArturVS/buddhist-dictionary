<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1062B

世尊聖者千眼千首千足千舌千臂觀自在菩提薩埵怛嚩廣大圓滿無礙大悲心陀羅尼
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1062B;

Shizun Shengzhe Qian Yan Qian Shou Qian Zu Qian She Qian Bi Guanzizai Putisaduo Da Fu Guangda Yuanman Wu'ai Dabei Xin Tuoluoni;

Great Compassionate Heart Dharani of the World Honored One, Thousand Eyes, Thousand Heads, Thousand Feet, Thousand Tongues, Thousand Arms, Avalokitesvara;

One scroll.

<h4>Summary</h4>
Siddham text for the Great Compassion Dharani.
