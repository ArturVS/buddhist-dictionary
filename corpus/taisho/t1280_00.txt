摩醯首羅大自在天王神通化生伎藝天女念誦法
 
共 1 卷

Volume 21, No. 1280;

Moxishouluo Dazizaitian Wang Shentong Huasheng Ji Yi Tiannu Niansong Fa;

Ritual Invoking Maheśvara’s Magical Transformation of Śṛīdevī;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manual group (1244–1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon version, Kyōhō era [1716-1736].
