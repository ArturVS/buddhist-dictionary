<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1287

大黑天神法

唐 神愷記

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1287;

Da Hei Tian Shen Fa;

Mahakala Ritual;

Recorded by Shen Kai in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A ritual to Mahakala, the Black Lord.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和二年刊長谷寺藏本, ＜甲＞承暦四年寫高山寺藏本, ＜乙＞天承四年寫高山寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, published second year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].
