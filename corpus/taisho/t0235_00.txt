<h4>Colophon</h4>
金剛般若波羅蜜經
後秦 鳩摩羅什譯

共 1 卷

Diamond Sūtra
Translated by Kumārajīva in the Later Qin.

<h4>Notes</h4>
The Diamond Sūtra, Sanskrit name Vajracchedikāprajñāpāramitāsūtra, was translated to Chinese in 401. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 13')
