<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1033

金剛恐怖集會方廣軌儀觀自在菩薩三世最勝心明王經

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1033;

Jingang Kongbu Jihui Fangguang Gui Yi Guanzizai Pusa San Shi Zui Sheng Xin Ming Wang Jing;

Fearsome Vajra Assembly Vaipulya Ritual, Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva Past, Present, and Future Supreme Heart Vidyaraja Sutra;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Avalokitesvara teaches recitation of great mantras. The text tells of the merits of the mantras, which can lead to attainments, and describes mudras. It is included within the 諸觀音儀軌類 Avalokitesvara ritual manuals (1030–1118) group.
