大佛頂如來放光悉怛多般怛羅大神力都攝一切呪王陀羅尼經大威德最勝金輪三昧呪品

共 1 卷

Volume 19, No. 947
Da Fo Ding Rulai Fang Guang Xi Da Duo Ban Da Luo Da Shenli Dou She Yiqie Zhou Wang Tuoluoni Jing Da Wei De Zui Sheng Jin Lun Sanmei Zhou Pin;

Great Usnisa Radiance Sitatapatra Divine Powers Collected Dharanis Sutra, Yamantaka Golden Wheel Samadhi Mantra;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The sitātapatra mentioned in the title is a white umbrella, a symbol of royalty and one of the 五佛頂 five crowns of the Buddha. This text is one of a group of 諸佛頂儀軌類 Buddha crown ritual manuals (944–981).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞延久三年寫東寺三密藏本

Source: Tō-ji Sanmitsu canon, 3rd year of the Enkyū era [1069-1074]
