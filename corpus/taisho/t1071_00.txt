共 1 卷

Volume 20, No. 1071
Avalokiteśvaraikādaśamukhadhāraṇīsūtra (Sutra of the Divine Spell of the Eleven-Faced)
Translated by Xuanzang in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 656 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 310')

English translations: None

<h4>Summary</h4>
Provdes the text of mantras, ritual procedure for reciting it, and the benefits. It also describes carving a wooden sculpture of Avalokiteśvara. The content is closely related in content to T 1069, T 1070, and T 1071.
