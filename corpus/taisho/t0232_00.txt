<h4>Colophon</h4>
文殊師利所說摩訶般若波羅蜜經
梁 曼陀羅仙譯

共 2 卷

Mañjuśrī speaks the Large Prajñāpāramitā Sūtra
Translated by Mandra in the Liang dynasty in 2 scrolls.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated by Mandra 曼陀羅仙 in 503, T 232 (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 10').

English translations: none
