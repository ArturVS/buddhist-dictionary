<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 956

大陀羅尼末法中一字心呪經

唐 寶思惟譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 956;

Great Dharani for the Age of Declining Dharma One Word Mantra Sutra (Da Tuoluoni Mofa Zhong Yizi Xin Zhou Jing);

Translated by Ratnacinta in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text is one of a group that describes a 一字 'one word' mantra, which is a magic word.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 705 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 424')
