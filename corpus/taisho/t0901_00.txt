<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 901

陀羅尼集經
唐 阿地瞿多譯

共 12 卷

Volume 18, No. 901
Dhāraṇīsamuccaya (Collected Dharanis Sutra)
Translated by Atigupta in the Tang in 12 scrolls

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 654 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 308')

English translations: None

<h4>Summary</h4>
Provdes the text of mantras, describes carving a wooden sculpture of Avalokiteśvara, and a mandala initiation ritual. Closely related in content to T 1069, T 1070, and T 1071.
