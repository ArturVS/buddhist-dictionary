Volume 18, No. 901,

Dhāraṇīsaṃgraha Sutra (Collected Dharanis Sutra),

Translated by Atikuta in the Tang in 12 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Provdes the text of mantras, describes carving a wooden sculpture of Avalokiteśvara, and a mandala initiation ritual. Closely related in content to T 1069, T 1070, and T 1071.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 654 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 308')
