<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1149

五大虛空藏菩薩速疾大神驗祕密式經
唐 金剛智譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1149;

Wu Da Xukong Zang Pusa Su Ji Da Shen Yan Mimi Shi Jing;

Scripture on the Five Great Akāśagarbha Bodhisattvas Rapid, Great Divine, and Secret Rite of Healing;

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes a ritual relating to the group of five Akāśgarbhas, including formation of the mandala and text of the dharanis.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version published in the 1st year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].
