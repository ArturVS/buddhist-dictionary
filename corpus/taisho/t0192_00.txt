Fo Suo Xing Zan (Buddhacarita)
Composed by Bodhisattva Aśvaghoṣa, translated by Dharmakṣema in 5 scrolls in the Northern Liang

Sanskrit title and date 414-426 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 980')

Enlgish translations: Beal, S. (1883) and Willemen, C. (2009)
