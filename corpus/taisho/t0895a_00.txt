<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 895a

蘇婆呼童子請問經

唐 輸波迦羅譯

共 3 卷

Volume 18, No. 895a

Supohu Tongzi Qingwen Jing

Subāhuparipṛcchā

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Questions of the Youth Subāhu; 蘇婆呼請問經 Questions of Subāhu; 蘇磨呼經 Subāhu Sūtra; 蘇磨呼經 Subahu Sutra; Tantra Requested by Subāhu; Subāhuparipṛcchātantra

<h4>Summary</h4>
Vajrapāṇi explains tantric practices to the boy Subāhu.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 726 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 428')

<h4>English translation</h4>
None
