Volume 21, No. 1345;

Vajramaṇḍādhāranīsūtra (Jingang Chang Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Jñānagupta in the Sui in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Sūtra of the Vajra Platform; Japanese: Kongō-jō-darani-kyō

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸陀羅尼經類 dharani sutras group (1331–1420).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 587 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 337')
