Volume 20, No. 1130;

Dasheng Jingang Jizhu Pusa Xiuxing Fen;

Mahayana Vajra Cudamani Bodhisattva Cultivation;

Translated by Bodhiruci in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
髻珠經 Cudamani Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text on meditation and bodhisattva cultivation in prose and heptasyllabic verse.
