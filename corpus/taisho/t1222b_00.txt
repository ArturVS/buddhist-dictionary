<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1222b

聖迦柅忿怒金剛童子菩薩成就儀軌經

唐 不空譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1222B;

Sheng Jia Ni Fennu Jingang Tongzi Pusa Chengjiu Yi Gui Jing;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243). See 1222A.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豐山大學藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736].
