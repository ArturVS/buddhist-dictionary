<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 954B

一字頂輪王念誦儀軌
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 954B;

Yizi Ding Lunwang Niansong Yi Gui;

One Syllable Buddhosnisa Wheel Turning Ruler Liturgy;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text is one of a group that describes a 一字 'one word' mantra, which is a magic word.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞明本, 此經麗宋元三本無之, ＜甲＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本 Source: Ming canon, this sutra does not appear in any of the three Korean canons from the Song and Yuan [dynasties] ; 1. Ōbaku Canon version with notes by Jōgon.
