Volume 12, No. 360
Sukhāvatīvyūhasūtra (Infinite Life Sutra)
Translated by Saṅghavarman in the Cao Wei in 2 scroll(s)

<h4>Notes</h4>
This is a popular Pure Land sūtra, also known as the <i>Larger Sutra on Amitāyus</i>.

Sanskrit title and date 252 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 26')

<h4>English translation</h4>
Inagaki and Stewart, 1995, pp. 1-62.
