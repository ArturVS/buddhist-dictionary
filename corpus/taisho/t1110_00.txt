<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1110

佛說一髻尊陀羅尼經

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1110;

Fo Shuo Yi Ji Zun Tuoluoni Jing;

Usnisa Sutra;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
一髻尊經 Usnisa Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the benefits of the Dharani, gives the text of the dharani, describes an abhiseka ritual, mandala, homa, and mudras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 746-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1360')
