<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1146

大虛空藏菩薩念誦法

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1146;

Recitation Methods of the Great Ākāśagarbha Bodhisattva;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1324')

English translations: None

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes esoteric rituals, including the fire ritual, also known as homa-siddhi.
