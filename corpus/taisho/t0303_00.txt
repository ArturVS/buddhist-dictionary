隋 闍那崛多譯

共 2 卷

Tathāgataguṇajñānācintyaviṣayāvatāranirdeśasūtra (Fo Huayan Ru Rulai De Zhi Bu Siyi Jingjie Jing)
Translated by Jñānagupta in the Sui in 2 scrolls
