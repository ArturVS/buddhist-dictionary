<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 920

佛心經

唐 菩提流志譯

共 2 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 920,

Fo Xin Jing,

Buddha Mind Sutra,

Translated by Bodhiruci in the Tang in 2 scrolls

<h4>Other names</h4>
佛心經品亦通大隨求陀羅尼 Buddha Mind Sutra Chapter and Wish Fulfillng Dharani

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the Dharani and mudra, also explaining their great power.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera canon version, published in the first year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].
