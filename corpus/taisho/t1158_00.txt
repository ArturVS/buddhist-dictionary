Volume 20, No. 1158;

Dizang Pusa Yi Gui;

Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva Liturgy;

Translated by Shupo Jialuo in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
A Ritual Manual on the Bodhisattva Dizang

<h4>Summary</h4>
Ksitigarbha teaches mantras. After Ksitigarbha recited the mantras divine changes take place, including flowers raining from the sky. Visualization, mudras, and other ritual procedures are described.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞永久五年寫東山三密藏本 Source: Buzan University Canon version published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Tōsan Sanmitsu Canon manuscript, fifth year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].
