<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1095

不空羂索呪心經

唐 菩提流志譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1095;

Bu Kong Juan Suo Zhou Xin Jing;

Amoghapāśa Heart Mantra Sūtra;

Translated by Bodhiruci in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains a version of the 母陀羅尼真言 Mother Dhāraṇī Mantra from T 1090.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞明本

Source: Ming Canon
