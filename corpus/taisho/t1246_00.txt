<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1246

摩訶吠室囉末那野提婆喝囉闍陀羅尼儀軌

唐 般若斫羯囉譯

共 1 卷

Volume 21, No. 1246;

Mohe Fei Shi Luo Mona Ye Ti Po He Luo She Tuoluoni Yi Gui;

Translated by Bore Zhuojieluo in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manuals group (1244–1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: 三十帖策子第二十二, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本, ＜乙＞寛喜三年寫寶壽院藏本, ＜丙＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本 Sanjuji sakushi, sheet 26; 2. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript; 3. Ōbaku Canon with annotations by Jōgon.
