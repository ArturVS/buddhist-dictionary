<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1322

新集浴像儀軌

唐 慧琳述

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1322;

Xin Ji Yu Xiang Yi Gui;

New Collection of Image Bathing Rituals;

Narrated by Hui Lin in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes bathing and making offerings to images of Buddhas and deities.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豐山大學藏本, ＜甲＞康平二年寫高山寺藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon version, published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736].
