佛頂尊勝心破地獄轉業障出三界祕密三身佛果三種悉地真言儀軌

唐 善無畏譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 906,

Fo Ding Zun Sheng Xin Po Diyu Zhuan Yezhang Chu San Jie Mimi San Shen Foguo San Zhong Xi Di Zhenyan Yi Gui,

Buddha Crown Worthy Supreme Heart able to Liberate from Hell, Transform Karmic Obstacles, and Transcend the Three Realms Secret Three Body Mantras for Three Types of Siddhi Ritual Manual,

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 1 scroll

Related to T 899.
