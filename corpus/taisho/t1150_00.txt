<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1150

轉法輪菩薩摧魔怨敵法

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1150;

Zhuan Falun Pusa Cui Mo Yuan Di Fa;

Dharmacakrapravartana Bodhisattva Destroys Mara Ritual;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha recites the Ten Syllable Usnisa Mudra Mantra for the protection of the ruler and the land.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本 Source: Ōbaku Canon version with annotations by Jōgon.
