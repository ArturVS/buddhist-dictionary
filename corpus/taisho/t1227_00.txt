Mahābalavajrakrodhasūtra (Da Weili Wu Shu Se Mo Ming Wang Jing);

Translated by Ajitasena in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243).
