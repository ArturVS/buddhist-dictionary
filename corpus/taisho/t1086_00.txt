<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1086

觀自在菩薩如意輪瑜伽

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1086;

Guanzizai Pusa Ruyi Lun Yujia;

Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva Mani Jewel Wheel Yoga;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
如意輪瑜伽 Mani Wheel Yoga; 觀自在菩薩如意輪瑜伽念誦法 Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva Mani Jewel Wheel Yoga Liturgy; 如意輪瑜伽念誦法 Mani Jewel Wheel Yoga Liturgy

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes an esoteric ritual performed within a mandala and gives dharanis. The symbolism of the six arms of Cintamanicakra Avalokitesvara is explained.
