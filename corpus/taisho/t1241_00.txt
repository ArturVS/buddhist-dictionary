<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1241

伽馱金剛真言
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1241;

Jiaduo Jingang Zhenyan;

Gadā Mantra;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞三十帖策子第二十六, ＜甲＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本 Source: Sanjuji sakushi, sheet 26; 2. Ōbaku Canon with annotations by Jōgon.
