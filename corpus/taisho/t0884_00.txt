Fo Shuo Mimi Xiang Jing

Guhyagarbharāja

Translated by Dānapāla and others in the Song in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.
