<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 949

奇特最勝金輪佛頂念誦儀軌法要

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 949;

Qite Zui Sheng Jin Lun Fo Ding Niansong Yi Gui Fa Yao;

Special Supreme Golden Wheel Great Usnisa Liturgy Summary;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the 一字頂輪王 One Letter Unsurpassed Wheel Turning King ritual.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞東寺三密藏古寫本, ＜甲＞石山寺藏古寫本 Source: Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon old manuscript; 1. Ishiyama-dera collection old manuscript.
