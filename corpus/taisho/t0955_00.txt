<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 955

一字頂輪王瑜伽觀行儀軌
唐 不空譯

共 1 卷

Volume 19, No. 955
Ekākṣaroṣṇīsacakrarājayogakalpa (Yizi Ding Lunwang Yujia Guan Xing Yi Gui)
Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 720-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1320')

<h4>English translations</h4>
None

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text is one of a group that describes a 一字 'one word' mantra, which is a magic word.
