<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1120B

勝初瑜伽儀軌真言

唐 空海請來

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1120B;

Sheng Chu Yujia Yi Gui Zhenyan;

Superior Yoga Ritual Mantra;

Requested of Kūkai in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Gives the text of a dharani in siddham script.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This work contains Siddham characters not displayed in the NTI Reader. Please see <a href='https://21dzk.l.u-tokyo.ac.jp/SAT/satdb2015.php'>SAT</a> to view them. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞寛治三年寫東寺三密藏本 Source: manuscript from the Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon, 3rd year of the Kanji era [1087-1094].
