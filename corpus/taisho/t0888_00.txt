<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 888

一切祕密最上名義大教王儀軌

宋 施護譯

共 2 卷

Volume 18, No. 888

Yiqie Mimi Zuishang Mingyi Da Jiao Wang Yi Gui

Sarvarahasyatantrarāja

Translated by Dānapāla in the Song in 2 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Sarvarahasya tantra

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 1009 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1452')
