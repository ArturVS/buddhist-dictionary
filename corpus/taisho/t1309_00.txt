<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1309

七曜星辰別行法

唐 一行撰

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1309;

Qi Yao Xingchen Bie Xing Fa;

Alternative Practice for the Seven Planets and Stars;

Composed by Yi Xing in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains a diagram of the night sky. Similar theme to T 1308.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和二年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, published in the second year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].
