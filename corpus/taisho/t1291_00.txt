<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1291

深沙大將儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1291;

Shensha Da Jiang Yi Gui;

Ritual for the Great General Shensha;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A ritual for Shensha, the King of Ghosts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞東寺寶菩提院三密藏藏本 Source: Tō-ji Hōbodai-in Sanmitsu Canon version.
