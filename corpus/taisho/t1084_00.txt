<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1084

觀世音菩薩如意摩尼輪陀羅尼念誦法

唐 寶思惟譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1084;

Guanshiyin Pusa Ruyi Moni Lun Tuoluoni Niansong Fa;

Translated by Ratnacinta in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Gives the text of the dharani and describes the esoteric ritual for chanting it, including mudras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞東寺三密藏古寫本

Source: Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon old manuscript.
