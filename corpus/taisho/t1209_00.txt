Volume 21, No. 1209;

Jingang Ding Yujia Jiang San Shi Chengjiu Ji Shen Mi Men;

Vajraśekhara-yoga's Very Highest and Secret Methods of Trailokyavijaya Siddhi;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
降三世極深密門 Secret Methods of Trailokyavijaya

<h4>Summary</h4>
Mantras and pentasyllabic Chinese verse for the recitation of the Trailokyavijaya Vidyaraja Ritual

Date 746-774 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1380')
