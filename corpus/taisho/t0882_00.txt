<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 882

佛說一切如來真實攝大乘現證三昧大教王經

宋 施護等譯

共 30 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 882,

Fo Shuo Yiqie Rulai Zhenshi She Dasheng Xian Zheng Sanmei Da Jiao Wang Jing,

Sarvatathāgatatattvasaṁgraha,

Translated by Dānapāla and others in the Song in 30 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Compendium of True Principles of all Tathāgatas

<h4>Summary</h4>
A full translation of the Sarvatathāgatatattvasaṃgraha; see also T 865.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 1012-1015 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1466')

<h4>English translation</h4>
None
