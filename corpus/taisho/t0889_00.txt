Volume 18, No. 889,

Yiqie Rulai Da Mimi Wang Weicengyou Zuishang Weimiao Da Mannaluo Jing,

Great Secret Wonderous Abdhutadharma Mandala of All Tathagatas Sutra,

Translated by Tian Xi Zai in the Song in 5 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
大祕密王曼拏羅經 Great Secret Mandala Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric yoga teaching describing mandala, consecration, and homa rituals; included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.
