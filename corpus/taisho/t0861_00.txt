<h4>Colophon</h4>

毘盧遮那五字真言修習儀軌

唐 不空譯

<br/>

Volume 18, No. 861,

Piluzhena Wu Zi Zhenyan Xiu Xi Yi Gui,

Vairocana Five Character Mantra Ritual Manual,

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Explains the meaning of the Five Character Mantra; a text included within the 大日經類 Vairocana Sutra group of sutras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taisho footnote for T 861 reads:

＜原＞延享元年刊大正大學藏本, ＜甲＞平安時代寫本高山寺藏豊山版

Source: transcribed into the Taisho University edition in the first year of the Enkyō era [1744-1748]; 1. a manuscript was transcribed into the Takayama Temple repository during the Heian Period Notes [794－1192].
