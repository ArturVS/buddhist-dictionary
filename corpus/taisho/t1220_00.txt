<h4>Summary</h4>
A text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243)

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豐山大學藏本, ＜甲＞寛治四年寫仁和寺藏本, ＜乙＞東寺三密藏古寫本 Source: Buzan University Canon version, published Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Ninna-ji collection manuscript, fourth year of the Kanji reign; 2. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon old manuscript.
