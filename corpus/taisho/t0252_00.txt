<h4>Colophon</h4>
普遍智藏般若波羅蜜多心經
唐 法月重譯

共 1 卷

Pubian Zhi Cang Boreluomiduo Xin Jing
Translated by Dharmacandra in the Tang in 1 scroll.

<h4>Notes</h4>
An alternate translation of the Heart Sūtra by Dharmacandra 法月 in 738. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 1267'; T 252)
