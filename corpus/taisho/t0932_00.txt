<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 932

金剛頂經瑜伽觀自在王如來修行法

唐 金剛智譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 932;

Jingang Ding Jing Yujia Guanzizai Wang Rulai Xiuxing Fa;

Vajra Pinnacle Sutra Yoga Lokesvararaja Tathagata Practice Ritual;

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛儀軌類 ritual texts for buddhas (918-943).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞明本

Source: Ming Canon
