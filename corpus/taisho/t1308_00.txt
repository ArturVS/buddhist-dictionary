<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1308

七曜攘災決

唐 金俱吒撰

共 2 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1308;

Qi Yao Rang Zai Jue;

Avoiding Misfortune Caused by the Seven Planets;

Compiled by Jinju Zha in the Tang in 2 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Relates to divining dangerous positons of the planets.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和二年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, published in the second year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].
