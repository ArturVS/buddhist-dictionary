大雲輪請雨經

唐 不空譯

共 2 卷

Volume 19, No. 989;

Mahāmeghasūtra (Da Yun Lun Qing Yu Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 2 scrolls

<h4>Other names</h4>
Sutra for the Rain of the Great Cloud; Japanese: Daiunrinseiukyō; Tibetan: sprin chen-po

<h4>Summary</h4>
See T 991.
