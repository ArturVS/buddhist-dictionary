<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 942

釋迦佛讚

清 達喇嘛薩穆丹達爾吉譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 942;

Shijia Fo Zan;

Sakyamuni Buddha Verses;

Translated by Dalamasamu Dandaerji in the Qing in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛儀軌類 ritual texts for buddhas (918-943).

<h4>Notes</h4>
A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞麗本

Source: Korean canon
