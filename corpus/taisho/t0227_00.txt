後秦 鳩摩羅什譯

Xiaopin Boreiluomi Sūtra or Perfection of Wisdom in Eight Thousand Lines
Translated by Kumārajīva in the Later Qin Kingdom (384－417) in 10 scrolls.

According to Lancaster the translation was performed in 408. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 7')

Enlgish translations: none

Source: CBETA

Link: <a href="http://tripitaka.cbeta.org/T08n0227">http://tripitaka.cbeta.org/T08n0227</a>

<h4>Notes</h4>
This sūtra, similar to the Sanskrit Aṣṭasāhasrikāprajñāpāramitāsūtra or Aṣṭa for short, is thought to be the earliest prajñāpāramitāsūtra sūtra. (Buswell and Lopez, 2014, 'Aṣṭasāhasrikāprajñāpāramitā')
