Volume 21, No. 1379;

Fo Shuo Da Ai Tuoluoni Jing;

Sea Spirit Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Faxian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A short dharani following a request for teaching of a sea spirit.
