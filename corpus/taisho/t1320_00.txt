<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1320

瑜伽集要焰口施食儀

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1320;

Yujia Ji Yao Yan Kou Shi Shi Yi;

Yoga Ritual for Offering Food to Flaming Mouth;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains Siddham script and images. See T 1313.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞明本, 此經麗宋元三本倶缺 Source: Ming Canon; this version differs considerably from the Korean, Song, and Yuan versions.
