<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 850

攝大毘盧遮那成佛神變加持經入蓮華胎藏海會悲生曼荼攞廣大念誦儀軌供養方便會

唐 輸婆迦羅譯

共 3 卷

Volume 18, No. 850

She Da Piluzhena Cheng Fo Shenbian Jia Chi Jing Ru Lianhua Tai Cang Hai Hui Bei Sheng Man Tu Luo Guangda Niansong Yi Gui Gongyang Fangbian Hui

Vairocana Sutra Lotus Platform Matrix Assembly Compassion Mandala Liturgy

Translated by Shupo Jialuo in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
攝大軌 Mahavairocana Ritual Manual; 攝大毘盧遮那念誦儀軌 Mahavairocana Liturgy

<h4>Summary</h4>
A chanting manual associated with the Vairocana Sutra (T 848).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon.
