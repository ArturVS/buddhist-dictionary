攝大毘盧遮那成佛神變加持經入蓮華胎藏海會悲生曼荼攞廣大念誦儀軌供養方便會

Volume 18, No. 850,

She Da Piluzhena Cheng Fo Shenbian Jia Chi Jing Ru Lianhua Tai Cang Hai Hui Bei Sheng Man Tu Luo Guangda Niansong Yi Gui Gongyang Fangbian Hui,

Vairocana Sutra Lotus Platform Matrix Assembly Compassion Mandala Liturgy,

Translated by Shupo Jialuo in the Tang in 3 scrolls,

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
攝大軌 Mahavairocana Ritual Manual; 攝大毘盧遮那念誦儀軌 Mahavairocana Liturgy

<h4>Summary</h4>
A chanting manual associated with the Vairocana Sutra (T 848).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A note in the Taishō reads, ＜原＞正徳元年刊豐山大學藏本, ＜甲＞平安時代寫高山寺藏本, ＜乙＞長元六年寫東寺三密藏本, 甲本此序無, 乙本首缺 Source: Buzan University Canon edition published in the first year of the Shōtoku era [1711]. 1. Takayama Temple edition copied in the Heian Period, 2. Tō-ji [temple] Sanmitsu edition copied in the sixth year of the Chōgen era [1028-1037], which lacks a preface present in the original.
