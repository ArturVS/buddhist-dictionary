Volume 18, No. 912,

Jianli Mantuluo Hu Mo Yi Gui,

Creating a Mandala for a Homa Ritual,

Compiled by Fa Quan in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of texts (908-914) relating to homa (fire) rituals and related procedures.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞豊山版大正大學藏本, ＜甲＞建久九年寫高山寺藏本, ＜乙＞長治二年寫東寺三密藏藏本

Source: Buzan edition, Taisho University repository; 1. transcrtion in the Takayama temple repository dated 9th year of the Kenkyū era [1190-1199], 2. Tō-ji sanmitsu canon manuscript version dated second year of the Chōji era [1104-1106]
