<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 924A

藥師如來念誦儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 924A,

Yaoshi Rulai Niansong Yi Gui,

Medicine Buddha Liturgy,

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛儀軌類 ritual texts for buddhas (918-943).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera temple canon published first year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804].

<h4>English translation</h4>
Salguero, C. Pierce 2017, “Healing Dhāraṇīs: A Collection of Medieval Spells from the Taishō Tripiṭaka,” In: P. Salguero (ed.), <i>Buddhism and Medicine - An Anthology of Premodern Sources</i>, New York: Columbia University Press, pp. 292-303.
