大般若波羅蜜多經
唐 玄奘譯

The Large Sūtra Perfection of Wisdom
Translated by Xuan Zang in the Tang

Volumes 5, 6, and 7 of the Taishō Tripiṭaka with a total of 600 scrolls.
Also known as the Mahāprajñāpāramitāsūtra, translation 660-663. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 1')

Enlgish translation from the Sanskrit: Conze, E., 1985. <i>The Large Sutra on Perfect Wisdom: With the Divisions of the Abhisamayalankara</i>. University of California Press.
