<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 983B

孔雀經真言等梵本

唐 圓仁請來

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 983B;

Mahāmāyūrīvidyārājñī (Kongque Jing Zhenyan Deng Fan Ben);

Requested of Ennin in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Great Peahen Queen of Spells Sanskrit Text

<h4>Summary</h4>
The Sanskrit text in Siddham script for T 983A; see also T 986.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text includes Siddhaṃ script that is not displayed properly. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞高野山高室院藏, ＜甲＞平安時代寫石山寺藏本 Source: Mount Kōya Takamuro-in collection; 1. Shishan Temple collection manuscript, Heian Period.
