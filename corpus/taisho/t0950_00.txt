<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 950

菩提場所說一字頂輪王經
唐 不空譯

共 5 卷

Volume 19, No. 950
Bodhimaṇḍanirdeśaikākṣaroṣṇiṣakakravartirājasūtra (Puti Changsuo Shuo Yizi Ding Lunwang Jing)
Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 5 scrolls

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 720-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1290')

<h4>English translations</h4>
None

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text describes a 一字 'one word' mantra, which is a magic word.
