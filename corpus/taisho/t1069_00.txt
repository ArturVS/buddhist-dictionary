十一面觀自在菩薩心密言念誦儀軌經
唐 不空譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1069;

Ritual Manuals for the Recitation of the Secret Mind Mantra of the Eleven-Faced Avalokiteśvara (Shiyi Mian Guanzizai Pusa Xin Miyan Niansong Yi Gui Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1286')

<h4>Summary</h4>
Provdes the text of mantras and describes visualization, mudras, homa (fire ritual), and carving a wooden sculpture of Avalokiteśvara. Closely related in content to T 901, T 1070, and T 1071.
