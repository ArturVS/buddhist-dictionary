Volume 18, No. 873,

Jingang Ding Lianhua Bu Xin Niansong Yi Gui,

Vajra Crown and Lotus Division Chanting Ritual Manual,

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
金剛界儀軌 Vajradhatu Ritual Manual; 蓮華部心儀軌 Lotus Division Heart Ritual Manual; 蓮華部儀軌 Lotus Division Ritual Manual

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes offerings to the deities in the Vajradhatu Mandala, including mudras and mantras; a text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.
