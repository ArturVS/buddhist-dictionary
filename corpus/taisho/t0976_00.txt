<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 976

佛頂大白傘蓋陀羅尼經

元 沙囉巴譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 976;

White Canopy Buddha Crown Dharani Sutra (Fo Ding Dabai Sangai Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Shaluoba in the monetary unit in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
白傘蓋陀羅尼經 White Umbrella Dharani Sutra; 白傘蓋經 White Umbrella Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the esoteric 白傘 White Canopy Buddha Crown dharani, ritual, and the virtue of performing it.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞元本 Source: Yuan version [Puning Canon].

<h4>English translations</h4>
None

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of three texts about the 白傘 White Canopy, one of the five 佛頂 Buddha Crowns
