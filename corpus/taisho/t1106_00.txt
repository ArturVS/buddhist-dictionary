讚揚聖德多羅菩薩一百八名經

宋 天息災譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1106;

Āryatārābhaṭṭarikāyanāmāṣṭottaraśatakasūtra (Zanyang Sheng De Duoluo Pusa Yibai Ba Ming Jing);

Translated by Tian Xi Zai in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A combination of Sanskrit mantra in Chinese transliteration and pentasyllabic Chinese verse. This text has different content than T 1105.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 985 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1120')
