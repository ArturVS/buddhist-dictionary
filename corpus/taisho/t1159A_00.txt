<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1159A

峚𡇪窖大道心驅策法
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1159A;

Da Dadao Xin Qu Ce Fa;

The Exorcism Method of Dizang’s Aspiration Toward Great Awakening;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Demons had taken their spirit essence of 500 people. After being empowered by the Buddha, Ksitigarbha recited a mantra to convert the demons to benevolent beings. Other details of the ritual are explained.

<h4>Notes</h4>
The character in the title represented in CBETA by 大+(企-止) has Unicode equivalent 𡇪 but there is no pronunciation given in the Unicode standard (Unihan s.v. '𡇪'). This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:  ＜原＞大日本續藏經 Source: Dai Nippon Zokuzōkyō [Supplementary Buddhist Canon].
