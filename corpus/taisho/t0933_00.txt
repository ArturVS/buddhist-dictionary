九品往生阿彌陀三摩地集陀羅尼經
唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 933;

Jiu Pin Wang Sheng Amituo Sanmodi Ji Tuoluoni Jing;

Nine Grades of Rebirth Amitabha Samadhi Assembly Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
九品往生經 Sutra on The Nine Grades of Rebirth; 九品往生阿彌陀經 Nine Grades of Rebirth Amitabha Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
Includes dharanis for rebirth in the Pure Land; considered apocryphal.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

享和元年刊長谷寺藏本

Hase-dera canon, published in the first year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804]
