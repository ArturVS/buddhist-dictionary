<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 966

大聖妙吉祥菩薩說除災教令法輪
唐 尸羅跋陀羅譯

共 1 卷

Volume 19, No. 966;

Da Sheng Miao Jixiang Pusa Shuo Chu Zai Jiao Ling Falun;

Great Holy Wonderful Luck Bodhisattva Discourses on the Removal of Calamities Causing the Dharma Wheel to Turn;

Translated by Shiluo Batuoluo in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Alternative names</h4>
Japanese: Daishō-myōkichijō-bosatsu-setsujosai-kyōryōhōrin

<h4>Summary</h4>
The primary text for the 熾盛光法 Blazing Light Ritual, a ritual to avoid disasters; one of the four great rituals of the Mountain School of the Tendai sect.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞貞元十二年寫高野山寶壽院藏本, ＜乙＞石山寺藏古寫本

Source: Buzan University canon published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. manuscript from Mount Kōya Hojuin Temple collection, 12th year of the Jōgen era [976-978]; 2. Ishiyama-dera collection old manuscript
