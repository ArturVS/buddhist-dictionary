<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 977

佛說大白傘蓋總持陀羅尼經

元 真智等譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 977;

Fo Shuo Dabai Sangai Zong Chi Tuoluoni Jing;

White Canopy Buddha Crown Summary Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Zhen Zhi and others in the Yuan in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of three texts about the 白傘 White Canopy, one of the five 佛頂 Buddha Crowns; see T 976.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞明本 Source: Ming Canon.
