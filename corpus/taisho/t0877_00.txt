<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 877

金剛頂經毘盧遮那一百八尊法身契印

唐 善無畏．一行譯

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 877

Jingang Ding Jing Piluzhena Yibai Ba Zun Fashen Qi Yin

Vajra Crown Tantra and Vairocana One Hundred and Eight Worthy Dharma Bodies and Mudras

Translated by Subhakarasimha ansd Yixing in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon.
