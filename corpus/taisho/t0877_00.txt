<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 877

金剛頂經毘盧遮那一百八尊法身契印

唐 善無畏．一行譯

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 877

Jingang Ding Jing Piluzhena Yibai Ba Zun Fashen Qi Yin

Vajra Crown Tantra and Vairocana One Hundred and Eight Worthy Dharma Bodies and Mudras

Translated by Subhakarasimha ansd Yixing in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains Siddham letters for changing and mudras; a text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞縮册大藏經, ＜甲＞仁平元年寫高山寺藏本, ＜乙＞保延三年寫高山寺藏本, ＜丙＞帖策子第二十, ＜丁＞黄蘗板淨嚴等校訂加筆本塚本賢曉氏藏

Source: Shukusatsu daizōkyō [Reduced Print Canon]; 1. transcribed into the Takayama temple canon in the first year of the Ninpei era [1151], 2. transcribed [again] into the Takayama temple canon in the third year of the Hōen era [1135-1141], 3. Jōgon added notes to the Ōbaku version comparing the Tsukamoto and ??? versions.
