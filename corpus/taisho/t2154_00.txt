<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 55 冊　No. 2154

開元釋教錄

唐 智昇撰

共 20 卷

Volume 55, No. 2154

Kaiyuan Shijiao Lu

Record of Buddhist Teachings Compiled During the Kaiyuan Era

Composed by Zhi Sheng in the Tang

20 scrolls

<h4>Other titles</h4>
開元錄 Kaiyuan Catalog

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text is a catalog containing the list of texts, thought to match the list of the scriptures in the 開寶藏 Kaibao Canon, first printed Chinese Buddhist canon.

<h4>Notes</h4>
