<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1085

觀自在菩薩如意輪念誦儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1085;

Guanzizai Pusa Ruyi Lun Niansong Yi Gui;

Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva Mani Jewel Wheel Liturgy;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
如意輪菩薩念誦法 Mani Wheel Bodhisattva Liturgy; 觀自在如意輪菩薩念誦法 Avalokitesvara Mani Wheel Bodhisattva Liturgy; 如意輪念誦儀軌 Mani Wheel Chanting and Ritual Manual

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes an esoteric ritual related to Cintamanicakra Avalokitesvara.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1323')
