佛說八部佛名經
元魏 瞿曇般若流支譯

共 1 卷

Volume 14, No. 429;
Sutra on the Names of the Eight Buddhas of the Eastern Quadrant (Fo Shuo Ba Bu Fo Ming Jing);
Translated by Maṅgalāṣṭaka in the Yuan Wei in 1 scroll(s)

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Aṣṭabuddhaka

<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha tells the names and the good qualities of the eight Buddhas of the Eastern quadrant.
