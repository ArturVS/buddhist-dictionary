<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1213

千臂軍荼利梵字真言

唐 義操記

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1213;

Qian Bi Jun Tu Li Fan Zi Zhenyan;

Thousand Arm Kundali Sanskrit Mantra;

Recorded by Yi Cao in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Siddham script for the mantra.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞石山寺藏古寫本 Source: Ishiyama-dera collection old manuscript.
