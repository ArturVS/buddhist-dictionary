Volume 20, No. 1116;

Guangda Lianhua Zhuangyan Man Na Luo Mie Yiqie Zui Tuoluoni Jing;

The Great Lotus Mandala that Erases and Sin Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Dānapāla in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Gives the story of the dharani, the text, the miraculous transformations from reciting it, and the details of the mandala and ritual.
