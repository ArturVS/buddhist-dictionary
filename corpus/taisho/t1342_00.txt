佛說無崖際總持法門經

Fo Shuo Wu Ya Ji Zong Chi Famen Jing;

Boundless Dharani Dharma Gate Sutra;

Translated by Shengjian in the Western Qin in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
無際經 Boundless Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸陀羅尼經類 dharani sutras group (1331–1420).
