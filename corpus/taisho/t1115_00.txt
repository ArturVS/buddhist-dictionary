<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1115

觀自在菩薩阿麼𪘨法
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1115;

Guanzizai Pusa Amezhai Fa;

Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva Abhetri Ritual;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Gives the text of the dharani and describes its merits.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞平安時代寫仁和寺藏本, ＜甲＞東寺三密藏古寫本 Source: manuscript from the Ninna-ji collection, Heian Period [794-1192]; 1. old manuscript from the Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon.
