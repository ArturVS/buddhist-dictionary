大日經持誦次第儀軌
 

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 860

Dari Jing Chi Song Cidi Yi Gui

Vairocana Sutra Chanting and Ritual Manual

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text passed down from the Shingon Buzan school, included within the 大日經類 Vairocana Sutra group of sutras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for T 860 reads:

＜原＞豊山版

Source: Buzan edition
