<h4>Colophon</h4>
光讚經
西晉 竺法護譯
共 10 卷

Guang Zan Jing
Translated by Dharmarakṣa in the Western Jin (265-316) in 10 scrolls.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated in 286. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 4')

English translations: none
