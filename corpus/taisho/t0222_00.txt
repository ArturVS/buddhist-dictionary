<h4>Colophon</h4>
光讚經
西晉 竺法護譯
共 10 卷
<br/>

In Praise of Light; Guang Zan Jing,
Translated by Dharmarakṣa in the Western Jin (265-316) in 10 scrolls.

<h4>Other titles</h4>
Perfection of Wisdom in Twenty-Five Thousand Lines; Pañcaviṃśatisāhasrikāprajñāramitāsūtra

<h4>Summary</h4>
A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition.

<h4>English Translations</h4>
Zacchetti, Stefano 2005, <i>In Praise of Light - A Critical Synoptic Edition with an Annotated Translation of Chapters 1–3 of Dharmarakṣa's Guang Zan Jing 光讚經, being the earliest Chinese Translation of the Larger Prajñāpāramitā</i>, Tokyo: The International Research Institute for Advanced Buddhology. Partial.
