Volume 21, No. 1383
Fo Shuo Suming Zhi Tuoluoni Jing;

Knowledge of Past Lives Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Faxian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
宿命智經 Knowledge of Past Lives Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
Teaches a dharani which can avoid all bad karmic retribution.
