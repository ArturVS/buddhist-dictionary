<h4>Summary</h4>
Jeweled Tower Sutra Mantra Sanskrit text. See T 1005A.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text includes Siddhaṃ script that is not displayed properly. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞承安元年寫東寺三密藏本 Source: manuscript from Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon, first year of the Jōan era [1171-1175].
