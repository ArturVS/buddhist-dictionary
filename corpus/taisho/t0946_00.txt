<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 946

大佛頂廣聚陀羅尼經

共 4 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 946;

Da Fo Ding Guang Ju Tuoluoni Jing;

Scripture on the Great Budoṣnīsa Extensive Collection of Dhāraṇīs;

4 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛頂儀軌類 Buddha crown ritual manuals (944–981).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Scroll 3 is missing. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞安元二年寫石山寺藏本, 五卷之中第三卷欠 Source: Manuscript from the Ishiyama-dera collection dated 2nd year of the Angen era [1175-1177], missing scroll no. 3 out of 5.
