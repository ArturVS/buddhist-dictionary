<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 1020

大方廣佛花嚴經入法界品頓證毘盧遮那法身字輪瑜伽儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 1020;

Dafangguang Fo Hua Yan Jing Ru Fajie Pin Dun Zheng Piluzhena Fashen Zi Lun Yujia Yi Gui;

Mahāvaipulya Avataṃsaka Sūtra Entrance into the Dharma Realm Chapter Vairocana Dharma Body Wheel of Characters Yoga Ritual Manual;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text contains an image of the wheel of charaters. See also T 1019.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞麗本

Source: Korean Canon.

<h4>English translation</h4>
Heng Tao, Bhikshuni, et al 1983, <i>Flower Adornment Sutra</i>, Talmage, CA: Buddhist Text Translation Society, pp. 86–87, Chap. 39, Part VII. Partial translation. Hard to find.
