<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1054

聖觀自在菩薩一百八名經

宋 天息災譯

共 1 卷

Volume 20, No. 1054;

Avalokiteśvaranāmāṣṭaśatakasūtra (Sheng Guanzizai Pusa Yibai Ba Ming Jing);

Translated by Tian Xi Zai in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text within the 諸觀音儀軌類 Avalokitesvara ritual manuals group (1030–1118).
