<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 904

念誦結護法普通諸部

唐 金剛智述

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 904

Niansong Jie Hufa Putong Zhu Bu

Chanting Ritual for Protection of the Body

Narrated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Ritual chanting for protection of the body against demons; the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本, ＜甲＞建長八年寫東寺三密藏本

Source: Hase-dera repository published in the first year of the Kyōwa era [1801]; 1. transcribed for the Tō-ji Sanmitsu repository in the eigth year of the Kenchō era [1249-1256]
