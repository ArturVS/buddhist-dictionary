聖六字增壽大明陀羅尼經

Volume 20, No. 1049;

Sheng Liu Zi Zeng Shou Da Ming Tuoluoni Jing;

Sacred Mantra of Six Syllables for Increasing Longevity Vidya Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Dānapāla in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text describes the merits of the Mantra of Six Syllables and use in avoiding misfortune.
