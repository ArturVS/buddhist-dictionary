十天儀軌
 
共 1 卷

Volume 21, No. 1296;

Shi Tian Yi Gui;

Ten Heavens Ritual Manual;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manual group (1244–1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞仁和寺藏古寫本 Source: Old manuscript from the Ninna-ji collection.
