<h4>Colophon</h4>

妙吉祥平等觀門大教王經略出護摩儀

宋 慈賢譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1194;

Miao Jixiang Pingdeng Guan Men Da Jiao Wang Jing Lue Chu Humo Yi;

Homa Ritual of the Auspicious Visualization Great Teaching Sutra;

Translated by Ci Xian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text in the 文殊儀軌類 Manjusri ritual manual group (1171-1198).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:  ＜原＞明本 Source: Ming Canon.
