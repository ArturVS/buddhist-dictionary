佛說俱枳羅陀羅尼經

宋 法賢譯

Volume 21, No. 1385;

Fo Shuo Juzhiluo Tuoluoni Jing;

Sutra on the Kasyapa Dharani;

Translated by Faxian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸陀羅尼經類 dharani sutras group (1331–1420).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 989-999 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1224a')
