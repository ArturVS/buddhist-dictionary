<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1248

北方毘沙門天王隨軍護法真言

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1248;

Bei Fang Pishamen Tian Wang Sui Jun Hufa Zhenyan;

Vaisravana of the North Defends the Dharma with his Army Mantra;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text include Siddham script for a mantra. It is related to similar texts on Vaisravana in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manuals group (1244–1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, published first year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804]; 1. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript.
