最上大乘金剛大教寶王經

宋 法天譯

共 2 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1128;

Vajragarbharatnarājatantra sūtra (Zuishang Dasheng Jingang Da Jiao Bao Wang Jing);

Translated by Fa Tian in the Song in 2 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha explains the essentials of the Vajrayāna teaching and outline of the main ritual elements, including abhiseka and mandalas to Indrabodhi.

<h4>Notes</h4>
