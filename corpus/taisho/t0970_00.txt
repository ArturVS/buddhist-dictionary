<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 970

最勝佛頂陀羅尼淨除業障呪經

唐 地婆訶羅譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 970;

Sutra of the Buddha-Crown Superb Victory Dharani (Zui Sheng Fo Ding Tuoluoni Jing Chu Yezhang Zhou Jing);

Translated by Divākara in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Sarvadurgatipariśodhana-uṣṇīṣavijayadhāraṇī

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes a ritual for ceasing of calamities and curing of illness.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 676-688 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 322')
