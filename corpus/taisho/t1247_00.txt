<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1247

北方毘沙門天王隨軍護法儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1247;

Bei Fang Pishamen Tian Wang Sui Jun Hufa Yi Gui;

Vaisravana of the North Defends the Dharma with his Army;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manuals group (1244–1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本 Buzan University Canon version published second year of the Kyōhō era [1716-1736].
