Volume 20, No. 1191

Mañjuśrīmūlakalpa (Dafangguang Pusa Zangwen Shu Shi Ligen Ben Yi Gui Jing)

Translated by Tian Xi Zai in the Song in 20 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the maṇḍala of Mañjuśrī, the images contained within it, mudras, homa, and other ritual elements in 28 chapters.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 983 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1138')
