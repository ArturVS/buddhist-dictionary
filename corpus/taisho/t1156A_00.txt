<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1156A

大隨求即得大陀羅尼明王懺悔法

唐 惟謹述

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1156A;

Da Sui Qiu Ji De Da Tuoluoni Ming Wang Chanhui Fa;

The Great Wish-Fulfilling Dharani and Repentance Ritual;

Narrated by Wei Jin in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric repentance ritual. The text describes mudras and text in Chinese prose for the ritual.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和元年刊長谷寺藏本, ＜甲＞元久元年寫高山寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version published in the first year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804]; 1. Takayama Temple collection manuscript, first year of the Genkyū era [1204–1206].
