聖者文殊師利發菩提心願文

Volume 20, No. 1198;

Shengzhe Wenshushili Fa Pu Ti Xin Yuan Wen;

Sage Manjusri's Vow for Enlightenment;

Translated by Zhi Hui in the Yuan in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A short heptasyllabic verse on Manjusri's vow to attain enlightenment.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:  ＜原＞明本 Source: Ming Canon.
