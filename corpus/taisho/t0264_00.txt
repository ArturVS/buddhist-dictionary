<h4>Colophon</h4>
添品妙法蓮華經
隋 闍那崛多共笈多譯

共 7 卷

The Lotus Sutra or Saddharmapuṇḍarīkasūtra (Tian Pin Miaofa Lianhua Jing)
Translated by Jñānagupta and Dharmagupta in 7 scrolls in the Sui

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 601-602 and Sanskrit title from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 118')

This is an early translation of the Lotus Sūtra.

English translations: none
