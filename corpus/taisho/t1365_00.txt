Volume 21, No. 1365;

Ba Ming Pu Mi Tuoluoni Jing;

Group of Eight Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Xuanzang in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
八名經 Group of Eight Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
Relates to the group of eight esoteric deities of the 金剛部 Vajra Division.
