Volume 32, No. 1666,
Treatise on the Awakening of Faith in the Mahāyāna,
Composed by Aśvaghoṣa, translated by Paramārtha in the Liang in 1 scroll

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 553 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 616'). See Buswell and Lopez for a discussion of the background on this text (Buswell and Lopez 2014, s.v. 'Dasheng qixin lun').

<h4>Summary</h4>
Also known as the 起信論 <i>Treatise on the Awakening of Faith</i>, for short.
This is the popular version of the 《大乘起信論義記》 <i>Notes on the Meaning of Treatise on the Awakening of Faith in the Mahāyāna</i> by Fazang (<a href="/taisho/t1846.html">T 1846</a>).
