<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 32 冊　No. 1666

大乘起信論
馬鳴菩薩造 梁 真諦譯

共 1 卷

Volume 32, No. 1666
Treatise on the Awakening of Faith in the Mahāyāna
Composed by Aśvaghoṣa, translated by Paramārtha in the Liang in 1 scroll

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 553 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 616').
See Buswell and Lopez for a discussion of the background on this text (Buswell and Lopez 2014, s.v. 'Dasheng qixin lun').
Also known as the 起信論 'Treatise on the Awakening of Faith', for short.
This is the popular version of the 《大乘起信論義記》 <i>Notes on the Meaning of Treatise on the Awakening of Faith in the Mahāyāna</i> by Fazang (<a href="/taisho/t1846.html">T 1846</a>).

English translations: 
(1) Aśvaghoṣa and Yoshito S. Hakeda 2006, <i>The Awakening of Faith: Attributed to Aśvaghoṣha</i>, Translations from the Asian Classics. New York: Columbia University Press.
