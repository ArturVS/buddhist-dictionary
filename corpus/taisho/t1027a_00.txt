金剛光焰止風雨陀羅尼經

Volume 19, No. 1027a
Jingang Guang Yan Zhi Fengyu Tuoluoni Jing;

The Vajra Blazing Light Stops Rainstorms Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Bodhiruci in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text within the 諸經儀軌類 sutra ritual manuals group (982–1029).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text contains Unicode characters that are not shown correctly in the NTI Reader. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞麗本, 此經宋明二本與麗本大異故別載之 Source: Korean canon; this text does not appear in the Song or Ming canons and differs greatly from the Korean canon.
