供養儀式

Volume 18, No. 859,

Gongyang Yishi,

Offering Rituals,

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Descriptions of mudras and rlated ritual procedures.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. The text includes Siddham characters and pictures of mudras. A Taishō footnote for T 859 reads: ＜原＞縮刷大藏經, ＜甲＞承安五年玄證傳受本高山寺藏本, ＜乙＞平安時代寫高山寺藏本 Source: Shukusatsu daizōkyō (Reduced Print Canon); 1. mysteriously recorded as recieved into to Takayama Temple edition in the fifth year of the Jōan era [1171-1175], 2. transcribed into the Takayama Temple edition in the Heian Period [794－1192].
