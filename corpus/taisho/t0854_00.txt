<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 854

胎藏梵字真言

 失譯

共 2 卷

Volume 18, No. 854

Tai Cang Fan Zi Zhenyan

Womb Matrix Sanskrit Mantra

Translated by Unknown

2 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains Siddham characters for mantras in the 大日經類 Vairocana Sutra.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taisho footnote for the text reads:

＜原＞平安時代寫興然本東寺三密藏藏本, ＜甲＞平安時代淳祐寫石山寺藏本

Source: Transcribed by Kōzen of Tō-ji [temple] from the Sanmitsu edition in the Heian Period. 1. Transcribed by Shunnyū from the Ishiyama-dera edition in the Heian Period
