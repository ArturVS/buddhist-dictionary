<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1259

摩利支天一印法
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1259;

Marichi Mudra Ritual;

Mo Li Zhi Tian Yi Yin Fa

<h4>Summary</h4>
Includes Siddham script for two mantras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: 享保年間刊豊山大學藏本 Buzan College Canon version, published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736].
