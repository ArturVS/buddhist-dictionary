<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 972

佛頂尊勝陀羅尼念誦儀軌法

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 972;

Fo Ding Zun Sheng Tuoluoni Niansong Yi Gui Fa;

Pinnacle Dharani of the Supreme Honored One;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes a ritual including the 尊勝曼荼羅 Supreme Mandala; this text is one of a group of 諸佛頂儀軌類 Buddha crown ritual manuals (944–981).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1314')
