<h4>Colophon</h4>
聖八千頌般若波羅蜜多一百八名真實圓義陀羅尼經
宋 施護等譯

共 1 卷

Prajñapāramitānāmāṣṭaśataka
Translated by Dānapāla and others in the Song in 1 scroll.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A dhāraṇī in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated by Dānapāla 施護 and others in the Song, T 230 (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 1455').

English translations: none
