佛說施一切無畏陀羅尼經

宋 施護等譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1373;

Sarvābhayapradānadhāraṇīsūtra (Fo Shuo Shi Yiqie Wuwei Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Dānapāla and others in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Tells of the Buddha's travel to Āmrapāli in Magadha where, at the request of Sakra, he teaches the Dharani of No Fear.

<h4>Notes</h4>
