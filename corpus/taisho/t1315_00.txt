Volume 21, No. 1315;

Shi Zhu E Gui Yin Shi Ji Shui Fa;

Method of Bestowing Drink and Food to Feed Hungry Ghosts;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains Siddham script.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: 

＜原＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本, ＜甲＞三十帖策子第二十六
