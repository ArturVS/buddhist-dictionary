道行般若經
後漢 支婁迦讖譯

共 10 卷

Daoxing Bore Jing or Practice of the Way
Translated by Lokakṣema in the Later Han (25-220) in 10 scrolls.

This is first translation of the Perfection of Wisdom in Eight Thousand Lines or the Aṣṭasāhasrikā Prajñāpāramitā into Chinese by Lokakṣema in 179. (Conze, 1973, p. xi; Lancaster, 2004, 'K 6') This sūtra is thought to be the earliest Prajñāpāramitāsūtra and Mahāyāna sūtra translated into Chinese. (Lancaster, 1975; Buswell and Lopez, 2014, 'Aṣṭasāhasrikāprajñāpāramitā')

Enlgish translation: Conze translated a Sanskrit version of this sūtra. (Conze, 1973)
