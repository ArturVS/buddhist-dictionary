佛說幻化網大瑜伽教十忿怒明王大明觀想儀軌經

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 891

Fo Shuo Huanhua Wang Da Yujia Jiao Shi Fennu Ming Wang Da Ming Guan Xiang Yi Gui Jing

Net of Illusions Great Yoga Ten Wrathful Wisdom Kings Contemplation Ritual Manual

Translated by Faxian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.
