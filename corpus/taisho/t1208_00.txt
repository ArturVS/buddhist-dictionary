<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1208

俱力迦羅龍王儀軌

唐 金剛智譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1208;

Julijialuo Long Wang Yi Gui;

Dragon King Kulika Ritual;

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
See 1206. This text includes Siddhaṃ script for the mantras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: 平安時代寫東寺寶菩提院三密藏藏本 Tō-ji Hōbodai-in Sanmitsu Canon manuscript, Heian Period [794-1192].
