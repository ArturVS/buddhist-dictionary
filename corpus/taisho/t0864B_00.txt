胎藏金剛教法名號

唐 義操集

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 864B,

Tai Cang Jingang Jiaofa Minghao,

Names of the Womb Matrix and Vajra Teachers,

Compiled by Yi Cao in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text included within the 大日經類 Vairocana Sutra group of sutras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for the text reads: ＜原＞正應二年寫東寺三密藏藏本, ＜甲＞大日本續藏經 Source: Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon manuscript, 2nd year of the Shōō era [1288-1293]; 1. Dai Nippon Zokuzōkyō.
