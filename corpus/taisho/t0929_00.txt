<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 929

淨瑠璃淨土摽
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 929;

Jing Liuli Jing Tu Biao;

Lapis lazuli Pure Land Signs;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes a ritual devoted to the Medicine Buddha and colors of items used.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞長治二年寫東寺寶菩提院藏本 Source: Tō-ji [temple] Hōbodai-in collection manuscript dated 2nd year of the Chōji era [1104-1106].
