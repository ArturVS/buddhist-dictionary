Volume 18, No. 890,

Fo Shuo Yujia Da Jiao Wang Jing,

Māyājālamahātantra,

Translated by Faxian in the Song in 5 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Ritual manual for worship to Mārīcī, included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 989 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1220')
