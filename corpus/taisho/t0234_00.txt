<h4>Colophon</h4>
佛說濡首菩薩無上清淨分衛經
宋 翔公譯

共 2 卷

Nāgaśrīpāripṛcchāsūtra
Translated by Xiang Gong in the Liu Song dynasty in 2 scrolls.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated by Xiang Gong 420-479, T 234. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 12')

English translations: none
