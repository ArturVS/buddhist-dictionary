<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 999

佛說守護大千國土經

宋 施護譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 999;

Mahāsahasrapramardanasūtra (Fo Shuo Shouhu Da Qian Guotu Jing);

Translated by Dānapāla in the Song in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric ritual for the blessing and protection of the state, including mantras for avoiding disasters.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 983 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1096')
