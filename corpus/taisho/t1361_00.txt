<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1361

六門陀羅尼經論

世親菩薩造

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1361;

Liu Men Tuoluoni Jing  Lun;

Explanation of the Sutra of the Dharani of Six Gates;

Composed by Vasubandhu in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A commentary on the Saṇmukhīdhāraṇīsūtra (T 1360).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞唐代寫巴里國民圖書館藏燉煌本, ＜甲＞唐代寫倫敦大英博物館藏燉煌本 Source: Tang Dynasty Dunhuang manuscript from the Bibliothèque nationale de France, Paris; 1. Tang Dynasty Dunhuang manuscript from the colleciton of the British Museum, London.
