<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1419

佛說造像量度經解

清 工布查布譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1419;

Fo Shuo Zaoxiang Liangdu Jing Jie;

Defining the Image - Measurements in Image-Making;

Translated by Gongbuchabu in the Qing in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains images and instructions on creating Buddhist artwork.

<h4>English translation</h4>
Cai, Jingfeng 2000, <i>The Buddhist Canon of Iconometry</i>, Ulm: Fabri Verlag, p. 143.

<h4>Parallels</h4>
Willemen, Charles 2006, <i>Defining the Image - Measurements in Image-Making</i>, India: Sri Satguru Publications.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞大日本續藏經 Source: Dai Nippon Zokuzōkyō [Supplementary Buddhist Canon].
