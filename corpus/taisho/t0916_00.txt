Volume 18, No. 916,

Shou Wu Jie Ba Jie Wen,

Text for Taking the Five Precepts and Eight Precepts, one scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Ceremony for taking the Five Precepts and Eight Precepts and also repentance and transfer of merit.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞縮刷大藏經, ＜甲＞黄蘗版淨嚴等校訂加筆本塚本賢曉氏藏

Source: Shukusatsu daizōkyō [Reduced Print Canon]; 1. Ōbaku version with collation notes by Jōgon and others from a stack of documents courtesy of the Akatsuki family repository
