<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 872

金剛頂瑜伽三十七尊出生義
唐 不空譯

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 872

Jingang Ding Yujia San Shi Qi Zun Chusheng Yi

The Meaning of the Birth of the Thirty-Seven Worthies in the Diamond Pinnacle Yoga

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the thirty-seven deities layed out in the 金剛界曼荼羅 vajradhatu-mandala. A text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞縮刷大藏經, ＜甲＞保延三年寫高山寺藏本, ＜乙＞鎌倉時代寫仁和寺藏本, ＜丙＞黄蘗版淨嚴等校訂加筆本塚本賢曉氏藏

Source: Shukusatsu daizōkyō [Reduced Print Canon]; 1. Transcribed into the Ninna-ji canon in the third year of the Hōen era [1135-1141], 2. Jōgon added notes to the Ōbaku version comparing the Tsukamoto and ??? versions.
