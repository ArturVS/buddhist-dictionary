Volume 20, No. 1132;

Jingang Wang Pusa Mimi Niansong Yi Gui;

Hevajra Secret Liturgy;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Explains the merits of the chanting practice and gives the verses.
