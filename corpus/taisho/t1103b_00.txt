<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1103b

觀自在菩薩怛嚩多唎隨心陀羅尼經

唐 智通譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1103b;

Guanzizai Pusa Da Fu Duo Li Sui Xin Tuoluoni Jing;

Scripture on Dhāraṇī of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva Dharma-Bhṛkutī According with the Heart;

Translated by Zhi Tong in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
隨心咒 Responsive Heart Mantra

<h4>Summary</h4>
Related but different to T 1103A. A text within the 諸觀音儀軌類 Avalokitesvara ritual manuals group (1030–1118).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text contains Siddham characters and images not displayed in the NTI Reader. Please see <a href='https://21dzk.l.u-tokyo.ac.jp/SAT/satdb2015.php'>SAT</a> to view those.
