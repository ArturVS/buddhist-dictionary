<h4>Colophon</h4>

大毘盧遮那成佛神變加持經略示七支念誦隨行法

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 856

Da Piluzhena Cheng Fo Shenbian Jia Chi Jing Lue Shi Qi Zhi Niansong Suixing Fa

Vairocana Sutra Seven Branches Short Liturgy

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text included within the 大日經類 Vairocana Sutra group of sutras.
