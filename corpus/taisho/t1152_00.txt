Volume 20, No. 1152;

Fo Shuo Fomu Boreboluomiduo Da Ming Guan Xiang Yi Gui;

Goddess Prajnaparamita Vidya Visualization Ritual;

Translated by Dānapāla in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Mantras in Siddham script and Chinese transliteration for short dharanis, some of one, two, and several syllables.
