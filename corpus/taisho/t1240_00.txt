<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1240

阿吒薄[牛*句]付囑呪

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1240;

A Zha Bao Hou Chouyou Zhou;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243).

<h4>Notes</h4>
The character represented as [牛*句] in CBETA in the title is expressed with Unicode 𤘽 hǒu. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本 Source: Buzan University Canon version published Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript.
