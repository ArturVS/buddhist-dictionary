<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1273

毘那夜迦誐那鉢底瑜伽悉地品祕要

唐 含光記

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1273;

Pinayejia Enabodi Yujia Xidi Pin Mi Yao;

Esoteric Essentials of the Yoga-Siddhi of the Vinayaka Ganapati;

Recorded by Han Guang in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of texts on Gaṇeśa (T 1267 - 1275); includes Siddham script for mantras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享和二年刊長谷寺藏本, ＜甲＞長寛三年刊高山寺藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, published second year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804]; 1. Takayama Temple Canon version, published in the third year of the Chōkan era [1163-1165].
