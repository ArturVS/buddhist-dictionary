<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 997

守護國界主陀羅尼經

唐 般若共牟尼室利譯

共 10 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 997;

Shouhu Guo Jie Zhu Tuoluoni Jing;

Protection of the Ruler of the State Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Prajña and Muniśrī in the Tang in 10 scrolls

<h4>Alternative Namaes</h4>
守護國界經 Protection of the State Sutra; 守護經 Protection of the State Sutra; Japanese: Shugo kokkaishu daranikyō

<h4>Summary</h4>
A ritual for the protection of the ruler of the state, mainly dharanis.

<h4>Notes</h4>
