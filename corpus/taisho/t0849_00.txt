<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 849

大毘盧遮那佛說要略念誦經

唐 菩提金剛譯

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 849

Da Piluzhena Fo Shuo Yao Lue Niansong Jing

Mahavairocana Buddha Chanting Summary

Translated by Puti Jingang in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An abbridged version of Scroll 7 of Vairocana Sutra (T 848).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A note in the Taisho regards

＜原＞大日本縮刷大藏經, 原本冠註曰此經依五部祕經載之
