金剛頂瑜伽最勝祕密成佛隨求即得神變加持成就陀羅尼儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1155;

Jingang Ding Yujia Zui Sheng Mimi Cheng Fo Sui Qiu Ji De Shenbian Jia Chi Chengjiu Tuoluoni Yi Gui;

Vajra Pinnacle Yoga, the Supreme Secret of Buddhahood, Wish-Fulfilling Divine Transformation and Empowerment Dharani;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
