<h4>Colophon</h4>

阿唎多羅陀羅尼阿嚕力經

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1039;

Ali Duoluo Tuoluoni Aluli Jing;

Arya-Tara Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Avalokitesvara recites the dhāraṇī and explains the merits accomplished.
