<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 926

藥師琉璃光王七佛本願功德經念誦儀軌供養法

元 沙囉巴譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 926;

Yaoshi Liuli Guang Wang Qi Fo Benyuan Gongde Jing Niansong Yi Gui Gongyang Fa;

Medicine Buddha, King of Lapis Lazuli Light and Merits of the Seven Buddhas Sutra and Liturgy for Offering Ritual;

Translated by Shaluoba in the Yuan in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛儀軌類 ritual texts for buddhas (918-943).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞元本

Source: Yuan Edition [Puning Canon]
