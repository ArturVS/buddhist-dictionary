<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1090

佛說如意輪蓮華心如來修行觀門儀

宋 慈賢譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1090;

Fo Shuo Ruyi Lun Lianhua Xin Rulai Xiuxing Guan Men Yi;

Cintāmaṇi Chakra Lotus Tathagata Cultivation and Contemplation Ritual;

Translated by Ci Xian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the teaching of the Cintamanicakra Avalokitesvara ritual with mantras and pentasyllabic verse to be chanted.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞明本 Source: Ming Canon.
