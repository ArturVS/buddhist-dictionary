<h4>Colophon</h4>

不空羂索陀羅尼經

唐 李無諂譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1096;

Bu Kong Juan Suo Tuoluoni Jing;

Amoghapāśa Dhāraṇī Sūtra;

Translated by Li Wuchan in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains a version of the 母陀羅尼真言 Mother Dhāraṇī Mantra from T 1090.

Date 700 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 291')
