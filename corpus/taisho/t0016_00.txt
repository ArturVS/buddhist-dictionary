<h4>Colophon</h4>
尸迦羅越六方禮經
後漢 安世高譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Sīgāla Pays Homage to the Six Directions
(Shijialuoyue Liu Fang Li Jing);
Translated by An Shigao in the Later Han in 1 scroll.


<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Śṛgālavādasūtra, 六方禮經 Sutra on the Ritual for the Six Directions; 尸迦羅越六向拜經 Sīgāla Pays Homage to the Six Directions

<h4>Notes</h4>
Translated in 148-170 (Lancaster 2004, 'K 656')
