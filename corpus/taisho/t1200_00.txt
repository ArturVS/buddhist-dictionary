<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1200

底哩三昧耶不動尊威怒王使者念誦法

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1200;

Trisamaya Recollection and Recitation Methods of the Worthy Immovable Wrathful King Envoy
(Di Li Sanmei Ye Budong Zun Wei Nu Wang Shizhe Niansong Fa);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Trisamayarāja, Trisamaya

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text describes esoteric rituals to defeat an opposing military force.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1285').
