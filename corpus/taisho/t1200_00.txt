<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1200

底哩三昧耶不動尊威怒王使者念誦法
唐 不空譯

共 1 卷

Volume 21, No. 1200
Trisamaya Recollection and Recitation Methods of the Worthy Immovable Wrathful King Envoy
(Di Li Sanmei Ye Budong Zun Wei Nu Wang Shizhe Niansong Fa)
Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Trisamayarāja, Trisamaya

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1285'). T 1201 is a closely related text.

<h4>English Translation</h4
Goble, G. C. 2019, <i>Chinese Esoteric Buddhism: Amoghavajra, the Ruling Elite, and the Emergence of a Tradition</i>, Play E-Book,   United States: Columbia University Press, pp. 129-133. Fragments.

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text describes esoteric rituals to defeat an opposing military force, including corpse re-animation.
