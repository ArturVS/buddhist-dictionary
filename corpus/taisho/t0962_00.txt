<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 962

寶悉地成佛陀羅尼經

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 962;

Bao Xi Di Cheng Fo Tuoluoni Jing;

Supernatural Powers and Enlightenment Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes a ritual, including mantras for attaining spiritual powers and enlightenment.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon published in the Kyōhō era.
