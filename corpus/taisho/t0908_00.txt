<h4>Colophon</h4>

金剛頂瑜伽護摩儀軌

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 908,

Homa Ritual Procedures of the Vajra Pinnacle Yoga,

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Identifyies and describes the different homa (fire ritual) practices.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1335')
