<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1230

佛說大輪金剛總持陀羅尼經

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1230;

Fo Shuo Da Lun Jingang Zong Chi Tuoluoni Jing;

Mahacakra Vajradhara Dharani Sutra;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞寛延二年刊豐山大學藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon version, second year of the Kan'en era [1748-1751].
