Volume 20, No. 1151;

Xiu Xi Boreboluomi Pusa Guan Xing Niansong Yi Gui;

Prajnaparamita Bodhisattva Cultivation, Visualization, and Chanting Ritual;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Mantras, pentasyllabic Chinese verse, and instructions in prose for the ritual.
