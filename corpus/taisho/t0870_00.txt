Volume 18, No. 870,

Lue Shu Jingang Ding Yujia Fenbie Sheng Wei Xiu Zheng Famen,

Summary of the Vajra Crown Yoga Teaching on Cultivation and Realization of Sagehood,

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Japanese: Ryakujutsu kongōchō yuga funbetsu shōi shūshō hōmon

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes ritual aspects of the Sarvatathāgatatattvasaṃgraha (T 865). A text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.
