Ratnaguṇasañcayagāthā
Translated by Fa Xian in the Song in 3 scrolls.

A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated by Fa Xian 法賢 in 991, T 229 (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 1200'). Literally, 'Verses on the Collection of Precious Qualities.' (BL, 'Ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthā')

Enlgish translations: none
