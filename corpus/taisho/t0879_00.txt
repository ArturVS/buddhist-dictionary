<h4>Colophon</h4>

金剛頂瑜伽三十七尊禮

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 879

Jingang Ding Yujia San Shi Qi Zun Li

Vajra Crown Yoga and Thirty Seven Deities Ritual

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon.
