<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 938

釋迦文尼佛金剛一乘修行儀軌法品

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 938;

Shijia Wen Ni Fo Jingang Yi Cheng Xiuxing Yi Gui Fa Pin;

Sakyamuni Buddha Vajra One Vehicle Liturgy;

One Scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes an esoteric ritual with Sakyamuni as the subject that will help overcome karmic obstacles and treat illness.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本, ＜乙＞高山寺藏證印本, ＜丙＞康和五年寫高野山寶壽院藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. old manuscript from the Takayama Temple collection; 2. printed copy from the Takayama Temple collection; 3. manuscript dated the 5th year of the Kōwa era [1099-1104] from the Mount Kōya Hojuin collection.
