<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1281

那羅延天共阿修羅王鬪戰法

唐 寶思惟譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1281;

Naluoyan tian Gong Axiuluo Wang Dou Zhan Fa;

Narayana and the King of the Asuras Battle Ritual;

Translated by Ratnacinta in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manual group (1244–1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞石山寺藏古寫本 Source: Ishiyama-dera collection old manuscript.
