唐 不空譯

共 1 卷

Volume 20, No. 1167;

Aṣṭamaṇḍalakasūtra (Ba Da Pusa Mantuluo Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
八大曼拏羅經 Scripture of the Eight Great Bodhisattvas Mandala

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the establishment of eight mandalas for the eight great bodhisattvas and ritual practices that can lead to enlightenment.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 746-774 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1304')
