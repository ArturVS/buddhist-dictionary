Volume 18, No. 881,

Xian Jie Shiliu Zun,

Sixteen Worthies of the Present Kalpa,

One Scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Lists each of the deities in the vajradhatu mandala of the present kalpa and gives mantras in Siddham letters; a text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞享和二年刊長谷寺藏本

Source: published in the second year of the Kyōwa era [1801-1804] in the Hase-dera canon.
