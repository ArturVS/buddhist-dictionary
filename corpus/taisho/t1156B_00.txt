<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1156B

宗叡僧正於唐國師所口受

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1156B;

Zongrui Seng Zheng Yu Tang Guoshi Suo Kou Shou;

Oral Transmission to Master Shūei while in the Tang;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes a ritual for the Mahāpratisarā Dhāraṇī transmitted to Japanese monastic Shūei [809-884] during his travel to China during the Tang dynasty.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞寛治七年寫高山寺藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Takayama Temple collection manuscript, seventh year of the Kanji era [1087-1094].
