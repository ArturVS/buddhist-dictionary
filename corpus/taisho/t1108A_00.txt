<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1108A

聖救度佛母二十一種禮讚經

元 安藏譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1108A;

Sheng Jiudu Fomu Ershi Yi Zhong Lizan Jing;

Sutra on the Twenty One Praises for the Sacred Goddess Tara;

Translated by An Zang in the Yuan dynasty in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Heptasyllabic Chinese verse with one short dharani.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:  ＜原＞明本 Source: Ming Canon.
