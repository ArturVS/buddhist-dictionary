第 21 冊　No. 1417

佛說壞相金剛陀羅尼經

元 沙囉巴譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1417;

Fo Shuo Huai Xiang Jingang Tuoluoni Jing;

Bhedalakṣaṇa Vajra Dhāraṇī Sutra;

Translated by Shaluoba in the Yuan in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸陀羅尼經類 dharani sutras group (1331–1420).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞元本, 此經麗宋明三本倶缺 Source: Yuan Canon [Puning Canon]; this text is missing from the Korean, Song, Ming canons.
