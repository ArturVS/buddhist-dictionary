<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 863

大毘盧遮那經阿闍梨真實智品中阿闍梨住阿字觀門

唐 惟謹述

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 863,

Da Piluzhena Jing Asheli Zhenshi Zhi Pin Zhong Asheli Zhu A Zi Guan Men,

Ācārya's True Knowledge Chapter of the Vairocana Sutra: Contemplation on the Letter A,

Narrated by Wei Jin in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A description of ritual meditation; a text included within the 大日經類 Vairocana Sutra group of sutras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for the text reads: ＜原＞大日本續藏經 Source: Dai Nippon Zokuzōkyō
