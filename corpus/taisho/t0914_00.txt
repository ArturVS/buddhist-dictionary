火吽軌別錄
 

共 1 卷

Volume 18, No. 914

Huo Hong Gui Bie Lu

Fire Hūṃ Ritual Summary

<h4>Summary</h4>
Mostly in Siddham letters with some figures of the mandala; one of a group of texts (908-914) relating to homa (fire) rituals and related procedures.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞豊山版, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本, ＜乙＞應保二年寫東寺三密藏本, 火吽軌別録＝火吽供養經法＜甲＞, 〔火吽軌別録〕－＜乙＞

Source: Buzan edition; 1. Takayama Temple repository ancient manuscript, 2. Tō-ji Sanitsu canon manuscript dated second year of the Ōhō era [1161-1163], ...

This Taisho text includes pictures and Siddham letters that  are not displayed properly in the NTI Reader.
