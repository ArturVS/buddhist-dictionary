Volume 19, No. 944B;

Da Fo Ding Da Tuoluoni;

Great Usnisa Dharani;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Contains the Siddhaṃ script of the dharani in T 944A

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text includes Siddhaṃ script that is not displayed properly. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞靈雲寺版普通眞言藏 Source: Reiunji [temple] version, common mantra collection.
