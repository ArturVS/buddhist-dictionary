Volume 19, No. 959;

Ding Lunwang Da Mantuluo Guan Ding Yi Gui;

Ritual Manual for Initiation into the Great Mandala of the Usnisa-Cakravartin

Compiled by Bianhong in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes a coronation ritual for a Cakravartin ruler.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞東寺三密藏古寫本 Source: Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon old manuscript.
