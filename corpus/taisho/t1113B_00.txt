大慈大悲救苦觀世音自在王菩薩廣大圓滿無礙自在青頸大悲心陀羅尼

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷

Volume 20, No. 1113B;

Da Ci Da Bei Jiu Ku Guanshiyin Zizai Wang Pusa Guangda Yuanman Wu'ai Zizai Qing Jing Dabei Xin Tuoluoni;

Avalokitesvara bodhisattva of Great Mercy, Nilakantha Great Compassion Dharani;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Gives the text of the dharani in Siddham and Chinese transliteration.
