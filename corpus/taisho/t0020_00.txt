吳 支謙譯

共 1 卷

Sutra on the Buddha's Liberation of the Brahmin Āmraṣṭha
(Fo Kaijie Fanzhi Aba Jing),
Translated by Zhi Qian in the Wu in 1 scroll.

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Ambaṭṭhasutta; 梵志阿颰經 Sutra on the Brahmin Āmraṣṭha; 阿颰摩納經 Āmraṣṭha Sutra; 阿拔經 Āmraṣṭha Sutra; 佛聞梵志阿颰經 The Buddha Visits the Brahmin Āmraṣṭha; 解梵志阿拔經 Sutra on the Liberation of the Brahmin Āmraṣṭha
