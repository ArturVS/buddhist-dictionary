<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1218

文殊師利耶曼德迦呪法

唐 宗叡將來

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1218;

Wenshushili Yemandejia Zhou Fa;

Yamāntaka Mantra Ritual;

Brought by Shūei in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha speaks with Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva and then recites the Yamāntaka Mantra.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞平安時代寫東寺寶菩提院三密藏藏本 Source: Tō-ji Hōbodai-in Sanmitsu Canon manuscript, Heian Period [794-1192].
