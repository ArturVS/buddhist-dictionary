金剛頂一字頂輪王瑜伽一切時處念誦成佛儀軌
唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 957;

Jingang Ding Yizi Ding Lunwang Yujia Yiqie Shi Chu Niansong Cheng Fo Yi Gui;

Vajra Crown One Word Wheel Yoga for All Time Periods Enlightenment Liturgy;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Japanese: Kongōchō ichiji chōrinnō yuga issai jisho nenju jōbutsu giki

<h4>Summary</h4>
The text is one of a group that describes a 一字 'one word' mantra, which is a magic word.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 746-774 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1358')
