Volume 21, No. 1269;

Fo Shuo Jinse Jia Na Bo Di Tuoluoni Jing;

The Golden Vināyaka Dhāraṇī Sūtra;

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of texts on Gaṇeśa (T 1267 - 1275)

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豐山大學藏本, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本, ＜乙＞保延元年寫東寺三密藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon version, published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript; 2. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon version, Hōen era [1135-1141].
