大明度經
吳 支謙譯

共 6 卷

Da Ming Du Jing
Translated by Zhi Qian in the Wu (222-280) in 6 scrolls.

This is second translation of the Perfection of Wisdom in Eight Thousand Lines or the Aṣṭasāhasrikā Prajñāpāramitā into Chinese by Zhi Qian in 222-229. (Conze, 1973, p. xi; Lancaster, 1975; Lancaster, 2004, 'K 9')

A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated 223-253. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 9')

Enlgish translations: Conze translated a Sanskrit version of this sūtra. (Conze, 1973)
