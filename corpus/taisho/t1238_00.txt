<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1238

阿吒婆𤘽鬼神大將上佛陀羅尼經

失譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1238;

A Zha Po Niu Ju Guishen Da Jiang Shang Fotuo Luo Ni Jing;

Spirit Commander Atavaka makes an Offering to the Buddha Dharani Sutra;

Translated by Unknown in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A collection of dhāraṇīs, expanded from T 1237, incorporating some from T 1332.  Includes images of mandalas and talismans as well as Siddham script.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞寶暦二年刊豐山大學藏本, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本, ＜乙＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本 Source: Buzan University Canon version published second year of the Hōreki era [1750-1765]; 1. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript; 2. Ōbaku Canon with annotations by Jōgon.
