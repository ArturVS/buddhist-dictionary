<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 851

大毘盧遮那經廣大儀軌

唐 善無畏譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 851,

Da Piluzhena Jing Guangda Yi Gui,

Vairocana Sutra Ritual Manual,

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 3 scrolls,

<h4>Summary</h4>
A collection of mantras associated with the Vairocana Sutra (T 848).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A note in the Taishō reads, ＜原＞正徳元年刊豐山大學藏本 Source: Buzan University Canon edition published in the first year of the Shōtoku era [1711-1716]
