受菩提心戒儀

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 915,

Shou Putixin Jie Yi,

Bodhicitta Precept Ceremony,

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A ritual manual describing tantric initiation precepts, including paying homage to the Buddha, repentance, refuge in the Triple Gem, arousal of bodhicitta, and precept conferral.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1331')
