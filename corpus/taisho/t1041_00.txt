<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1041

觀自在菩薩心真言一印念誦法

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1041;

Guanzizai Pusa Xin Zhenyan Yi Yin Niansong Fa;

Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva Heart Mantra and Mudra Liturgy;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text gives a mantra, describes related mudras, and Siddhaṃ script.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text includes Siddhaṃ script that is not displayed properly in the NTI Reader. This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞寛弘三年寫東寺三密藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon manuscript, 3rd year of the Kankō era [1004-1013].
