<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1267

使呪法經

唐 菩提留支譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1267;

Shi Zhou Fa Jing;

Sutra of the Mantras and Rituals of the Gana;

Translated by Bodhiruci in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Japanese: Shiju hokyō

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text connected with the deity Vināyaka, a form of Gaṇeśa. Includes Siddham script for the mantras.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本 Source: Ōbaku Canon version with annotations by Jōgon; 1. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript.
