<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 979

于瑟抳沙毘左野陀囉尼

高麗 指空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 979;

Yu Se Ni Sha Pi Zuo Ye Tuo Luo Ni;

Usnisa Dharani;

Translated by Dhyānabhadra in the Korean Goryeo dynasty in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text is one of a group of 諸佛頂儀軌類 Buddha crown ritual manuals (944–981).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞東熙三十七年刊眞言集東京帝國大學梵文研究室藏本

Source: Tokyo Imperial University Sanskrit Research Repository Dharani Collection, 17th year of the 東熙 era [can't find it]
