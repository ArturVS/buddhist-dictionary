囉嚩拏說救療小兒疾病經

宋 法賢譯

Volume 21, No. 1330;

Luofuna Shuo Jiu Liao Xiao Er Jibing Jing;

Sutra on Rāvaṇa's Teaching of the Spell for Curing Children;

Translated by Faxian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of texts relating to healing sickness (T 1324-1330).

Date 989-999 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1216')
