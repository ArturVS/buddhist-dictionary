<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 981

大毘盧遮那佛眼修行儀軌

唐 一行記

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 981;

Da Piluzhena Fo Yan Xiuxing Yi Gui;

Vairocana Buddha Eye Liturgy;

Recorded by Yi Xing in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text is one of a group of 諸佛頂儀軌類 Buddha crown ritual manuals (944–981).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本, ＜甲＞保安四年寫仁和寺藏本, ＜乙＞高山寺藏古寫本 Source: Buzan University Canon, published in the Kyōhō era; 1. Ninna-ji collection old manuscript, 4th year of the Hōan era [1120-1124]; 2. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript.
