Volume 18, No. 880,

Yujia Jingang Ding Jing Shi Zimu Pin,

Explanation of Characters in the Yoga Vajra Crown Tantra,

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Yoga-vajraśekhara-sūtra-aksharamatrika-vyākhyā-varga; Japanese: Yuga kongōchōgyō shaku jimobon

<h4>Summary</h4>
Gives a meaning to each letter of the Sanskrit alphabet; a text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.
