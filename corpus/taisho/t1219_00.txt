<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1219

曼殊室利焰曼德迦萬愛祕術如意法

唐 一行撰

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1219;

Manshushili Yan Man De Jia Wan Ai Mi Shu Ruyi Fa;

Wish Fulfilling Ritual from the Secret Methods of the Immeasurablly Compassionate Mañjuśrī-Yamāntaka;

Compiled by Yi Xing in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric ritual relating to Yamantaka with six heads, six arms, and six feet riding a water buffalo. The text contains a description of a mandala and images.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞保元二年寫高山寺藏本 Source: Takayama Temple collection manuscript, second year of the Hōgen era [1156-1159].
