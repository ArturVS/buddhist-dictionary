<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 941

釋迦牟尼佛成道在菩提樹降魔讚
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 941;

Verses on Sakyamuni Buddha's Enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree and Vanquishing of Mara;

Shijiamouni Fo Cheng Dao Zai Puti Shu Jiangmo Zan;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛儀軌類 ritual texts for buddhas (918-943).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞縮刷大藏經 Source: Shukusatsu daizōkyō [Reduced Print Canon].
