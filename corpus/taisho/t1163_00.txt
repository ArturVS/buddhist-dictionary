Volume 20, No. 1163;

Vasudhārādhāraṇī (Fo Shuo Yu Bao Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
妙月長者所問經 Sutra on the Questions of Elder Sucandra

<h4>Summary</h4>
Elder Sucandra asks the Buddha about obtaining wealth and curing disease. The text of the dharani in Siddham text and Sanskrit translation.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text includes Siddhaṃ script that is not displayed properly. Sanskrit title and date 746-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1295').
