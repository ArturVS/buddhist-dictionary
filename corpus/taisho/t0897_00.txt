<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 18 冊　No. 897

蕤呬耶經

唐 不空譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 897,

Guhya Sutra,

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
瞿醯壇怛囉 Guhyatantra

<h4>Summary</h4>
The tantra of secrets; included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞大日本縮刷大藏經, ＜甲＞石山寺藏古寫本, ＜乙＞保延四年寫高山寺藏本, ＜丙＞黄蘗板淨嚴等校訂加筆本, 《蕤呬･･･經》十二字＝《瞿醯壇跢羅二合經密法品巻上》十三字＜甲＞, 《瞿醯壇跢羅二合經序品第一巻上亦名蕤呬耶經》二十字＜乙＞, 蘗本作瞿醯壇跢羅經巻上亦名蕤呬耶經 Source: Shukusatsu daizōkyō [Reduced Print Canon]; 1. Ishiyama-dera repository ancient manuscript, 2. the Takayama temple version was transcribed in the 4th year of the Hōen era [1135-1141], 3. Jōgon and others added revision notes to the Ōbaku version, [details of some changes] ...
