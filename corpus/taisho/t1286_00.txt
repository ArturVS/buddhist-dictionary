<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1286

堅牢地天儀軌

唐 善無畏譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1286;

Jianlao Di Tian Yi Gui;

Prthivi Ritual Manual;

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
地天儀軌

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes offerings to the Earth Spirit Prthivi and Laksmi, including chanting, mantras, and homa. The ritual can increase longevity and wisdom with practice and has boundless merit.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豊山大學藏本 Source: Buzan College Canon version, published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736].
