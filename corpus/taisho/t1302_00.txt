Volume 21, No. 1302;

Zhu Xing Mu Tuoluoni Jing;

Dharani Sutra on the Mother of the Stars;

Translated by Fa Cheng in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manual group (1244–1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞唐代寫燉煌本 Source: Dunhuang Tang dynasty manuscript.
