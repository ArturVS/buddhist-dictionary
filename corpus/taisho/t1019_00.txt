<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 1019

大方廣佛華嚴經入法界品四十二字觀門

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 1019;

Dafang Guang Fo Huayan Jing Ru Fajie Pin Si Shi Er Zi Guan Men;

Method of Meditation on the Forty-two Letters of the Gaṇdhayūha Chapter of the Avataṃsaka Sūtra;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
四十二字觀門 Method of Meditation on the Forty-two Letters; 入法界品四十二字觀 Method of Meditation on the Forty-two Letters of the Gaṇdhayūha Chapter; 華嚴入法界四十二字觀門 Method of Meditation on the Forty-two Letters of the Gaṇdhayūha Chapter of the Avataṃsaka Sūtra

<h4>Summary</h4>
A retranslation of part of the 入法界品 Gaṇdhayūha Chapter of the Avataṃsaka Sūtra and an explanation of meditation on the letters.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 720-774 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1329')
