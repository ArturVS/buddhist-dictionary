<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1239

阿吒薄俱元帥大將上佛陀羅尼經修行儀軌

唐 善無畏譯

共 3 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1239;

A Zha Bao Ju Yuan Shuai Da Jiang Shang Fotuo Luo Ni Jing Xiuxing Yi Gui;

Translated by Śubhakarasiṃha in the Tang in 3 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Siddham script and Chinese transliteration for maantras and vidyārāja ritual text related to the vidyārāja Atavaka.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本, ＜甲＞高山寺藏古寫本, ＜乙＞石山寺藏古寫本 Source: Ōbaku Canon with annotations by Jōgon; 1. Takayama Temple collection old manuscript; 2. Ishiyama-dera collection old manuscript.
