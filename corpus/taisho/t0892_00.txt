佛說大悲空智金剛大教王儀軌經

宋 法護譯

共 5 卷
<br/>

Volume 18, No. 892,

Fo Shuo Dabei Kong Zhi Jingang Da Jiao Wang Yi Gui Jing,

Hevajratantra,

Translated by Dharmarakṣa in the Song in 5 scrolls

<h4>Other names</h4>
The Scriptural Text of the Ritual of the Great King of the Teaching, the Adamantine One with Great Compassion and Knowledge of the Void; Hevajraḍākinījālasaṃ varatantra; Hevajra ḍākinī-jala-saṃvara-tantra; Mahātantrarāja-māyākalpa

<h4>Summary</h4>
An anuttarayogatantra category of Tantric text depicting the central deity Hevajra as a naked siddha.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:

＜原＞明本, 東京帝國大學梵本三百三十五號, Hevajra－tantra

Source: Ming canon, Tokyo Imperial University Sanskrit text No. 335, Hevajra－tantra
