<h4>Colophon</h4>
正法華經
西晉 竺法護譯

共 10 卷

The Lotus Sutra or Saddharmapuṇḍarīkasūtra (Zheng Fa Hua Jing)
Translated by Dharmarakṣa in 10 scrolls in the Western Jin

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 286 and Sanskrit title from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 117')

This is an early translation of the Lotus Sūtra.

English translations: none
