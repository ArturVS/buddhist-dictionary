佛說無量壽佛化身大忿迅俱摩羅金剛念誦瑜伽儀軌法

唐 金剛智譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1223;

Fo Shuo Wuliang Shou Fo Huashen Da Fen Xun Ju Mo Luo Jingang Niansong Yujia Yi Gui Fa;

Translated by Vajrabodhi in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸儀軌類 vidyārāja ritual manuals group (1199–1243).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊豐山大學藏本 Source: Buzan University Canon published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736].
