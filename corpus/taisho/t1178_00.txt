<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1178

文殊菩薩獻佛陀羅尼名烏蘇吒
 
共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1178;

Wenshu Pusa Xian Fotuo Luo Ni Ming Wusuzha;

Manjusri Bodhisattva Offers the Buddha a Dharani named Usota;

One scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A short mantra in the 文殊儀軌類 Manjusri ritual manual group (1171-1198) of texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞三十帖策子第二十六, ＜甲＞黄檗版淨嚴等校訂加筆本 Source: Sanjuji sakushi, sheet 26.
