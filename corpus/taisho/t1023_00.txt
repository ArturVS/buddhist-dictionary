Volume 19, No. 1023;

Sarvatathāgatādhiṣṭhānahṛdayaguhyadhātukaraṇḍadhāraṇī (Yiqie Rulai Zhengfa Mimi Qie Yin Xin Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Dānapāla in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
The Scripture of the Mudrās and Dhāraṇīs of the Precious Repository of the Secret Whole-body Śarīra of all the Tathāgata

<h4>Summary</h4>
