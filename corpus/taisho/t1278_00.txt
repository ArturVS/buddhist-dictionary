<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1278

迦樓羅及諸天密言經
唐 般若力譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1278

Jialouluo Ji Zhutian Miyan Jing;

Scripture of Mantras of Garuḍa and Gods;

Translated by Prajnabala in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Advise on the medicine of Gāruḍas, including the treatment of snakebites.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞承安三年寫東寺三密藏本 Source: Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon version, third year of the Jōan era [1171-1175].
