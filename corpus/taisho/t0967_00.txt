佛頂尊勝陀羅尼經

唐 佛陀波利譯

Volume 19, No. 967

Usnisa Vijaya Dharani Sutra (Fo Ding Zun Sheng Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Buddhapāla in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Superlative Dhāraṇī of the Buddha’s Crown; 尊勝陀羅尼經 Usnisa Vijaya Dharani

<h4>Summary</h4>
Same as T 968 but with additional prefaces. See also T 970.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞麗本, ＜甲＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本, 此經増上寺元本缺, 此序麗宋二本倶無之今依明本載之 Source: Korean canon; 1. Ōbaku Canon with notes by Jōgon, not included in Zōjō-ji Puning Canon; the Korean canon does not have the prefaces included in this Ming Canon version.
