<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1304

宿曜儀軌

唐 一行撰

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1304;

Su Yao Yi Gui;

Ritual Manual of the Constellations and Celestial Objects;

Compiled by Yi Xing in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Enumerates the connections between Akasagarbha Bodhisattva, Manjusri Bodhisattva, Samantabhadra Bodhisattva and celestial bodies. It also gives mantras and mudras for various celestial bodies.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞享保年間刊長谷寺藏本, ＜甲＞康元二年寫東寺三密藏本 Source: Hase-dera Canon version, published in the Kyōhō era [1716-1736]; 1. Tō-ji Sanmitsu Canon manuscript, second year of the Kōgen era [1256-1257].
