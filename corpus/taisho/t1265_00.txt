<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1265

佛說常瞿利毒女陀羅尼呪經

唐 瞿多譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1265;

Fo Shuo Chang Ju Li Du Nu Tuoluoni Zhou Jing;

Dhāraṇī Sūtra of Jāṅgulī, the Girl able to Dispel Poison;

Translated by Ju Duo in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
See 1264A. This version also includes images of talismans for treating poison and dispelling demons and features on the palm of the hand.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞仁平二年寫石山寺藏本 Source: Ishiyama-dera collection manuscript, second year of the Ninpei era [1151-1154].
