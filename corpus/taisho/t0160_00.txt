<h4>Colophon</h4>
菩薩本生鬘論
宋 紹德慧詢等譯

共 16 卷

Pusa Bensheng Man Lun (Jātakamālā)
Translated by Shao De Hui Xin in 16 scrolls

<h4>Notes</h4>
A collection of 34 Jātaka stories written in Sanksrit composed by the poet śūra. The Chinese does not correspond well to the Sanskrit text. (Buswell and Lopez 2014, 'Jātakamālā')

English translations: From the Sanskrit (Āryaśūra and Speyer 1895)
