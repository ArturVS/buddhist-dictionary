摩訶般若波羅蜜大明呪經
姚秦 鳩摩羅什譯

共 1 卷

Mohe Boreluomi Da Ming Zhou Jing
Translated by Kumārajīva in the Later Qin in 1 scroll.
A version of the Heart Sūtra or Prajñāpāramitāhṛdayasūtra. The translation was performed 402-412. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 1190')
