<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1193

妙吉祥平等瑜伽祕密觀身成佛儀軌

宋 慈賢譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1193;

Miao Jixiang Pingdeng Yujia Mimi Guan Shen Cheng Fo Yi Gui;

Myohoji Equal Yoga Secret Body Observation and Buddhahood Ritual;

Wonderful, Auspicious Yoga Esoteric Visualization Ritual for Attainment of Buddhahood;

Translated by Ci Xian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
A text in the 文殊儀軌類 Manjusri ritual manual group (1171-1198).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads:  ＜原＞明本 Source: Ming Canon.
