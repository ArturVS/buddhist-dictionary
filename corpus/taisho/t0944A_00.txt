<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 944A

大佛頂如來放光悉怛多鉢怛囉陀羅尼

唐 不空譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 944A;

Da Fo Ding Rulai Fang Guang Xi Da Duo Bo Da Luo Tuoluoni;

Great Usnisa Radiance Sitatapatra Dharani;

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛頂儀軌類 Buddha crown ritual manuals (944–981).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞黄檗版淨嚴等加筆本 Source: Ōbaku Canon with additonal notes by Jōgon and others.
