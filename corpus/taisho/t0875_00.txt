Volume 18, No. 875,

Lianhua Bu Xin Niansong Yi Gui,

Lotus Division Chanting Ritual Manual,

Translated by Unknown in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Siddham letters for mantras, related to T 873; a text included within the 金剛頂經類 Vajraśekhara sūtra group of related texts.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞平安時代寫東寺三密藏藏本 Source: Tō-ji [temple] Sanmitsu Canon manuscript, Heian Period [794－1192].
