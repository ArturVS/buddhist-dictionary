Volume 20, No. 1037;

Samantabhadradhāraṇīsūtra (Guanzizai Pusa Shuo Puxian Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Avalokitesvara bodhisattva explains the merits of the Samantabhadra Dhāraṇī, enters the Vajra Mandala Samadhi and recites dhāraṇī. Following that the effects of the dhāraṇī are listed, including liberation from suffering and curing of illness, and more ritual details are given.
