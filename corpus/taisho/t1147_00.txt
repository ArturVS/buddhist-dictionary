聖虛空藏菩薩陀羅尼經

宋 法天譯

共 1 卷

Volume 20, No. 1147;

Saptabuddhakasūtra (Sheng Xukong Zang Pusa Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Fa Tian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Tells the story of the Buddha saving two suffering monastics by reciting the Dharani of the Seven Buddhas.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 984 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1106')
