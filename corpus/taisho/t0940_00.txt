<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 19 冊　No. 940

佛說帝釋巖祕密成就儀軌

宋 施護譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 19, No. 940;

Fo Shuo Dishi Yan Mimi Chengjiu Yi Gui;

Sakra's Lofty Esoteric Attainments Ritual Manual;

Translated by Dānapāla in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of 諸佛儀軌類 ritual texts for buddhas (918-943).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 1001 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1416')
