<h4>Colophon</h4>
十牛圖頌
宋 師遠述
共 1 卷

Verses of the Pictures of the Ten Ox
By Song dynasty monk Shi Yuan

<h4>Notes</h4>
Verses that accompany the well known pictures of herding ox.
